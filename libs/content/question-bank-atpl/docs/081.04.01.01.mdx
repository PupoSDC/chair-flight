# Basics and definitions

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.04.01.01.01** -- Define "static stability": describe/identify a
    statically stable, neutral, and unstable condition (positive, neutral, and
    negative static stability), and explain why aeroplanes are statically
    stable.
2.  **081.04.01.01.02** -- Explain manoeuvrability.
3.  **081.04.01.01.03** -- Explain the relationship between static stability and
    manoeuvrability.
4.  **081.04.01.01.04** -- Define "dynamic stability": describe/identify a
    dynamically stable, neutral, and unstable motion (positive, neutral, and
    negative dynamic stability); describe/identify periodic and aperiodic
    motion.
