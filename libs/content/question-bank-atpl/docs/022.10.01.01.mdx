# Definitions and transmission modes

## Learning Objectives

1.  **022.10.01.01.01** -- Describe the purpose of a data-link transmission
    system.
2.  **022.10.01.01.02** -- Compare voice communication versus data-link
    transmission systems.
3.  **022.10.01.01.03** -- Describe the communication links that are used in
    aircraft: high-frequency (HF) communications; very high-frequency (VHF)
    communications; satellite communications (SATCOM).
4.  **022.10.01.01.04** -- Consider the properties of the communication links
    with regard to: signal quality; range/area coverage; range; line-of-sight
    limitations; quality of the signal received; interference due to ionospheric
    conditions; data transmission speed.
5.  **022.10.01.01.05** -- Define and explain the following terms in relation to
    aircraft data-link communications: message/data uplink; message/data
    downlink.
