# Aircraft magnetic field

## Learning Objectives

1.  **022.03.02.01.01** -- Explain the following differences between permanent
    magnetism and electromagnetism: when they are present; what affects their
    magnitude.
2.  **022.03.02.01.02** -- Explain the principles of and the reasons for:
    - compass swinging (determination of initial deviations);
    - compass compensation (correction of deviations found);
    - compass calibration (determination of residual deviations).
3.  **022.03.02.01.03** -- Explain how permanent magnetism within the aircraft
    structure and electromagnetism from the aircraft systems affect the accuracy
    of a compass.
4.  **022.03.02.01.04** -- Describe the purpose and the use of a deviation
    correction card.

    as E/W or +/â€“) from a compass deviation card to calculate: compass heading
    to magnetic heading; magnetic heading to compass heading.
