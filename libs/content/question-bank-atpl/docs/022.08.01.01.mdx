# Design and operation

## Learning Objectives

1.  **022.08.01.01.01** -- Explain the purpose of the trim system and describe
    the layout with one trim system for each control axis, depending on the
    complexity of the aircraft.
2.  **022.08.01.01.02** -- Give examples of trim indicators and their function,
    and explain the significance of a "green band/area" for the pitch trim.
3.  **022.08.01.01.03** -- Describe and explain an automatic pitch-trim system
    for a conventional aeroplane.
4.  **022.08.01.01.04** -- Describe and explain an automatic pitch-trim system
    for an FBW aeroplane and that it is also operating during manual flight;
    however, during certain phases it may be automatically disabled to alter the
    handling characteristics of the aircraft.
5.  **022.08.01.01.05** -- Describe the consequences of manual operation on the
    trim wheel when the automatic pitch-trim system is engaged.
6.  **022.08.01.01.06** -- Describe and explain the engagement and disengagement
    conditions of the autopilot according to trim controls.
7.  **022.08.01.01.07** -- Define "Mach trim" and state that the Mach-trim
    system can be independent.
8.  **022.08.01.01.08** -- Describe the implications for the pilot in the event
    of a runaway trim or significant out-of-trim state.
