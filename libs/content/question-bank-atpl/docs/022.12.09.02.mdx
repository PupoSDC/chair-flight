# Terrain-avoidance warning system (TAWS); other name: enhanced GPWS (EGPWS)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **022.12.09.02.01** -- Explain the purpose of a TAWS for aeroplanes and of a
    HTAWS for helicopters, and explain the difference from a GPWS.
2.  **022.12.09.02.02** -- Explain inputs and outputs of a TAWS/HTAWS and
    describe its working principle.
3.  **022.12.09.02.03** -- Give examples of terrain displays and list the
    different possible alerts.
4.  **022.12.09.02.04** -- Give examples of time response left to the pilot
    according to look-ahead distance, speed and aircraft performances.
5.  **022.12.09.02.05** -- Explain why the TAWS/HTAWS must be coupled to a
    precise-position sensor.
6.  **022.12.09.02.06** -- Explain the possibility of triggering spurious
    TAWS/HTAWS warnings as a result of mismanaging the flight path in the
    proximity to obstacles: high rate of descent; high airspeed; a combination
    of high rate of descent and high airspeed.
