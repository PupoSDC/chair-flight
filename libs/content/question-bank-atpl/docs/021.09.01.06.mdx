# Electromagnetism

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.09.01.06.01** -- State that an electrical current produces a magnetic
    field.
2.  **021.09.01.06.02** -- Describe how the strength of the magnetic field
    changes with the magnitude of the current.
3.  **021.09.01.06.03** -- Explain the purpose and the working principle of a
    solenoid.
4.  **021.09.01.06.04** -- Explain the purpose and the working principle of a
    relay.
5.  **021.09.01.06.05** -- Explain the principle of electromagnetic induction
    and how two electrical components or systems may affect each other through
    this principle.
