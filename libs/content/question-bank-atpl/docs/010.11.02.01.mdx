# SAR - Organisation - Establishment and provision

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.11.02.01.01** -- Describe how ICAO Contracting States shall arrange
    for the establishment and prompt provision of SAR services.
2.  **010.11.02.01.02** -- Explain the establishment of SAR by Contracting
    States.
3.  **010.11.02.01.03** -- Describe the areas within which SAR services shall be
    established by Contracting States.
4.  **010.11.02.01.04** -- State the period of time per day within which SAR
    services shall be available.
5.  **010.11.02.01.05** -- Describe for which areas rescue coordination centres
    shall be established.
