# Arriving and departing aircraft

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.07.02.15.01** -- List the elements of information which shall be
    transmitted to an aircraft as early as practicable if an approach for
    landing is intended.
2.  **010.07.02.15.02** -- List the elements of information to be transmitted to
    an aircraft at the commencement of final approach.
3.  **010.07.02.15.03** -- List the elements of information to be transmitted to
    an aircraft during final approach.
4.  **010.07.02.15.04** -- State the prerequisites for operating on parallel or
    near-parallel RWYs including the different combinations of parallel arrivals
    or departures.
5.  **010.07.02.15.05** -- State the sequence of priority between aircraft
    landing (or in the final stage of an approach to land) and aircraft
    intending to depart.
6.  **010.07.02.15.06** -- State the significant changes in the meteorological
    conditions in the take-off or climb-out area that shall be transmitted
    without delay to a departing aircraft.
7.  **010.07.02.15.07** -- State the significant changes that shall be
    transmitted as early as practicably possible to an arriving aircraft,
    particularly changes in the meteorological conditions.
