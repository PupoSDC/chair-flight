# Airspace, communication, visual and radio-navigation data from VFR charts

## Learning Objectives

1.  **033.01.01.01.01** -- Select routes taking the following criteria into
    account: classification of airspace; restricted areas; VFR semicircular
    rules; visually conspicuous points; radio-navigation aids.
2.  **033.01.01.01.02** -- Find the frequencies or identifiers of
    radio-navigation aids from charts.
3.  **033.01.01.01.03** -- Find the communication frequencies and call signs for
    the following: control agencies and service facilities; flight information
    service (FIS); weather information stations; automatic terminal information
    service (ATIS).
