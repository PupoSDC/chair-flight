# Altimeter-setting procedures

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.07.02.09.01** -- Define the following terms: TRL; transition layer;
    and TA.
2.  **010.07.02.09.02** -- Describe how the vertical position of an aircraft in
    the vicinity of an AD shall be expressed at or below the TA, at or above the
    TRL, and while climbing or descending through the transition layer.
3.  **010.07.02.09.03** -- Describe when the HGT of an aircraft using QFE during
    an NDB approach is referred to the landing THR instead of the AD elevation.
4.  **010.07.02.09.04** -- State in which margin altimeter settings provided to
    aircraft shall be rounded up or down.
5.  **010.07.02.09.05** -- Describe the expression "lowest usable FL".
6.  **010.07.02.09.06** -- Determine how the vertical position of an aircraft on
    an en-route flight is expressed at or above the lowest usable FL and below
    the lowest usable FL.
7.  **010.07.02.09.07** -- State who establishes the TRL to be used in the
    vicinity of an AD.
8.  **010.07.02.09.08** -- Decide how and when a flight crew member shall be
    informed about the TRL.
9.  **010.07.02.09.09** -- State whether or not the pilot can request TRL to be
    included in the approach clearance.
