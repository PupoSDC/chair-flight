# Barometric pressure, isobars

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.01.03.01.01** -- Define "atmospheric pressure".

    pressure used in aviation (hPa, inches of mercury). (Refer to Subject 050 10

    barometer, aneroid barometer).

    charts.
5.  **050.01.03.01.05** -- Define "high", "low", "trough", "ridge", "col".
