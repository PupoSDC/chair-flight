# Steady level flight

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.01.03.01.01** -- Explain how drag (thrust) and power required vary
    with speed in straight and level flight.
2.  **032.01.03.01.02** -- Explain the effect of excess thrust and power on
    speed in level flight.
3.  **032.01.03.01.03** -- Interpret the "thrust/power required" and
    "thrust/power available" curves in straight and level flight.
4.  **032.01.03.01.04** -- Describe how the maximum achievable straight and
    level flight IAS and TAS vary with altitude.
5.  **032.01.03.01.05** -- Describe situations in which a pilot may elect to fly
    for "maximum endurance" or "maximum range".
