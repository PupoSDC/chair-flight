1.  **022.01.02.01.01** -- Explain temperature.
2.  **022.01.02.01.02** -- List the following units that can be used for
    temperature measurement: Kelvin; Celsius; Fahrenheit.
3.  **022.01.02.01.03** -- State the relationship between these units and
    convert between them.
4.  **022.01.02.01.04** -- Identify temperature measurements that are applicable
    to an aircraft: gas temperature measurement (ambient air, bleed-air systems,
    air-conditioning systems, air inlet, exhaust gas, gas turbine outlets);
    liquid-temperature measurement (fuel, oil, hydraulic); component-temperature
    measurement (generator, transformer rectifier unit (TRU), pumps (fuel,
    hydraulic), power transfer unit (PTU).
5.  **022.01.02.01.05** -- Identify and read temperature measurement indications
    for both engine indications and other systems.
