# RNAV 1/RNAV 2/RNP 1/RNP 2

## Learning Objectives

1.  **062.07.05.03.01** -- State that pilots must not fly an RNAV 1, RNAV 2, RNP
    1 or RNP 2 standard instrument departure (SID) or standard instrument
    arrival (STAR) unless it is retrievable by route name from the on-board
    navigation database and conforms to the charted route.
2.  **062.07.05.03.02** -- State that the route may subsequently be modified
    through the insertion (from the database) or deletion of specific waypoints
    in response to ATC clearances.
3.  **062.07.05.03.03** -- State that the manual entry, or creation of new
    waypoints by manual entry, of either latitude and longitude or
    place/bearing/distance values is not permitted.
