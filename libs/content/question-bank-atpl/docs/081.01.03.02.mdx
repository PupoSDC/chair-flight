# The lift coefficient (CL)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.01.03.02.01** -- Explain the lift formula, the factors that affect
    lift, and perform simple calculations.
2.  **081.01.03.02.02** -- Describe the effect of camber on the CL-alpha graph
    (symmetrical and positively/negatively cambered aerofoils).
3.  **081.01.03.02.03** -- Describe the typical difference in the CL-alpha graph
    for fast and slow aerofoil design.
4.  **081.01.03.02.04** -- Define "CLMAX" (maximum lift coefficient) and
    "alphaCRIT" (stalling alpha) on the graph.
5.  **081.01.03.02.05** -- Describe CL and explain the variables that affect it
    in low subsonic flight.
