# Certificate of Airworthiness (CofA) - Details

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.02.02.01.01** -- State the issuing authority of a CofA.
2.  **010.02.02.01.02** -- State the necessity to hold a CofA.
3.  **010.02.02.01.03** -- Explain the prerequisites for the issue of a CofA
    according to Commission Regulation (EU) No 748/2012.
4.  **010.02.02.01.04** -- State who shall determine an aircraft"s continuing
    airworthiness.
5.  **010.02.02.01.05** -- Describe how a CofA can be renewed or may remain
    valid.
