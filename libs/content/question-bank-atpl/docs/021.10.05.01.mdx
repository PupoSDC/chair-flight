# Design, operation, indications and warnings

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.10.05.01.01** -- Specify the reasons for cooling a piston engine.
2.  **021.10.05.01.02** -- Describe the design features to enhance cylinder air
    cooling for aeroplanes.
3.  **021.10.05.01.03** -- Describe the design features to enhance cylinder air
    cooling for helicopters (e.g. engine-driven impeller and scroll assembly,
    baffles).
4.  **021.10.05.01.04** -- Compare the differences between liquid- and
    air-cooling systems.
5.  **021.10.05.01.05** -- Identify the cylinder head temperature indication to
    monitor engine cooling.
6.  **021.10.05.01.06** -- Describe the function and the operation of cowl
    flaps.
