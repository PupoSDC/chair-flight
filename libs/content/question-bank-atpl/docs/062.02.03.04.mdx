# Errors and accuracy

## Learning Objectives

1.  **062.02.03.04.01** -- Define that the accuracy the pilot has to fly the
    required bearing in order to be considered established on a VOR track when
    flying approach procedures, according to ICAO Doc 8168, has to be within the
    half-full scale deflection of the required track.
2.  **062.02.03.04.02** -- State that due to reflections from terrain, radials
    can be bent and lead to wrong or fluctuating indications, which is called
    "scalloping".
