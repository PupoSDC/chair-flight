# Anticyclones, types, general properties, cold and warm anticyclones, ridges and subsidence

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.07.02.01.01** -- List the different types of anticyclones.
2.  **050.07.02.01.02** -- Describe the effect of high-level convergence in
    producing areas of high pressure at ground level.
3.  **050.07.02.01.03** -- Describe air-mass subsidence, its effect on the
    environmental lapse rate, and the associated weather.
4.  **050.07.02.01.04** -- Describe the formation of warm and cold anticyclones.
5.  **050.07.02.01.05** -- Describe the formation of ridges.
6.  **050.07.02.01.06** -- Describe the properties of and the weather associated
    with warm and cold anticyclones.
7.  **050.07.02.01.07** -- Describe the properties of and the weather associated
    with ridges.
8.  **050.07.02.01.08** -- Describe the blocking anticyclone and its effects.
