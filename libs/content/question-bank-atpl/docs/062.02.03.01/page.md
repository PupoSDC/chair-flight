  </Option>

    West
  </Option>

  <Explanation>
    A VOR works by sending two signals. A 30Hz reference signal is sent omni
    directional and serves as a reference. A second directional signal is sent
    by a rotating limacon at a frequency of 30Hz. The phase difference of these
    2 signals can be measured. Conveniently, the pase difference corresponds to
    the degrees magnetic we are from the station, i.e.: a phase difference of
    270º means we are west of the station.
  </Explanation>
