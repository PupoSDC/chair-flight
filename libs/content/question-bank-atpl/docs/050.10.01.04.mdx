# Weather radar observations (Refer to Subject 050 09 04 05.

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.10.01.04.01** -- Describe the basic principle and the type of
    information given by a ground weather radar.
2.  **050.10.01.04.02** -- Interpret ground weather radar images.
3.  **050.10.01.04.03** -- Describe the basic principle and the type of
    information given by airborne weather radar.
4.  **050.10.01.04.04** -- Describe the limits and the errors of airborne
    weather radar information.
5.  **050.10.01.04.05** -- Interpret typical airborne weather radar images.
