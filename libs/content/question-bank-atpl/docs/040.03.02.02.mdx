# Mental models and situation awareness

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.03.02.02.01** -- Define the term "situation awareness".
2.  **040.03.02.02.02** -- List the cues that indicate loss of situation
    awareness and name the steps to regain it.
3.  **040.03.02.02.03** -- List the factors that influence one"s situation
    awareness both positively and negatively, and stress the importance of
    situation awareness in the context of flight safety.
4.  **040.03.02.02.04** -- Define the term "mental model" in relation to a
    surrounding complex situation.
5.  **040.03.02.02.05** -- Describe the advantages/disadvantages of mental
    models.
6.  **040.03.02.02.06** -- Explain the relationship between personal "mental
    models" and the creation of cognitive illusions.
