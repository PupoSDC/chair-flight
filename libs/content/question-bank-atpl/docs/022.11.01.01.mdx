# Purpose, architecture, failures, functions

## Learning Objectives

1.  **022.11.01.01.01** -- Explain the purpose of an FMS.
2.  **022.11.01.01.02** -- Describe a typical dual FMS architecture including
    the following components: flight management computer (FMC); CDU/MCDU;
    cross-talk bus.
3.  **022.11.01.01.03** -- Describe the following failures of a dual FMS
    architecture and explain the potential implications to the pilots: failure
    of one FMC; failure of one CDU/MCDU; failure of the cross-talk bus.
4.  **022.11.01.01.04** -- Describe how the FMS integrates with other systems
    and gathers data in order to provide outputs depending on its level of
    complexity.
5.  **022.11.01.01.05** -- Explain how the FMS may provide the following
    functions: navigation; lateral and vertical flight planning; performance
    parameters.
