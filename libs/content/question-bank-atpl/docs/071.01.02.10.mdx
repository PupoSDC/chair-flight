# Cabin crew/crew members other than flight crew

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.01.02.10.01** -- Explain who is regarded as cabin crew member.
2.  **071.01.02.10.02** -- Detail the requirements regarding the number and
    composition of cabin crew.
3.  **071.01.02.10.03** -- Explain the conditions and the additional conditions
    for assignment to duties.
4.  **071.01.02.10.04** -- Explain the requirements regarding senior cabin crew
    members.
5.  **071.01.02.10.05** -- Explain the conditions for operating on more than one
    aircraft type or variant.
6.  **071.01.02.10.06** -- Explain what is the operator"s responsibility
    regarding the distinction between cabin crew members and additional crew
    members.
