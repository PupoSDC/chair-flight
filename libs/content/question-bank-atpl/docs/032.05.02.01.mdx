# Drift-down and stabilising altitude (performance data)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.05.02.01.01** -- Determine the one-engine-out net stabilising altitude
    (level-off altitude) from given graphs/tables.
2.  **032.05.02.01.02** -- Determine the maximum mass at which the net
    stabilising altitude with one-engine-out clears the highest relevant
    obstacle by the required clearance margin.
3.  **032.05.02.01.03** -- Determine, using drift-down graphs, fuel used, time
    and distance travelled in a descent from a cruise flight level to a given
    altitude.
