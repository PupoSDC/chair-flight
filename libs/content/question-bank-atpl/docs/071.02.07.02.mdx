# Actions to avoid and actions to take when encountering wind shear

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.02.07.02.01** -- Describe the effects of wind shear and the actions
    required when wind shear is encountered at take-off and approach.
2.  **071.02.07.02.02** -- Describe the precautions to be taken when wind shear
    is suspected at take-off and approach.
3.  **071.02.07.02.03** -- Describe the effects of wind shear and the actions
    required following entry into a strong downdraft wind shear.
4.  **071.02.07.02.04** -- Describe a microburst and its effects.
