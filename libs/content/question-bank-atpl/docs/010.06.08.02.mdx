# Operation of airborne collision avoidance system (ACAS) equipment

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.06.08.02.01** -- Describe the main reason for using ACAS.
2.  **010.06.08.02.02** -- State whether the "use of ACAS indications" described
    in ICAO Doc 8168 is absolutely mandatory.
3.  **010.06.08.02.03** -- Explain the pilots" reaction required to allow ACAS
    to fulfil its role of assisting pilots in the avoidance of potential
    collisions.
4.  **010.06.08.02.04** -- Explain why pilots shall not manoeuvre their aircraft
    in response to traffic advisories (TAs) only.
5.  **010.06.08.02.05** -- Explain the significance of TAs in view of possible
    resolution advisories (RAs).
6.  **010.06.08.02.06** -- State why a pilot should follow RAs immediately.
7.  **010.06.08.02.07** -- List the reasons which may force a pilot to disregard
    an RA.
8.  **010.06.08.02.08** -- Explain the importance of instructing ATC immediately
    that an RA has been followed.
9.  **010.06.08.02.09** -- Explain the duties of a pilot with regard to ATC when
    an RA situation is resolved.
