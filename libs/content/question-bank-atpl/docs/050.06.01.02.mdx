# Modifications of air masses

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.06.01.02.01** -- List the environmental factors that affect the final
    properties of an air mass.
2.  **050.06.01.02.02** -- Explain how maritime and continental tracks modify
    air masses.
3.  **050.06.01.02.03** -- Explain the effect of passage over cold or warm
    surfaces.
4.  **050.06.01.02.04** -- Explain how air-mass weather is affected by the
    season, the air-mass track and by orographic and thermal effects over land.
5.  **050.06.01.02.05** -- Assess the tendencies of the stability of an air mass
    and describe the typical resulting air-mass weather including the hazards
    for aviation.
