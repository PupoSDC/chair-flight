# Average wind velocity (WV)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **061.01.08.02.01** -- WV used for climb problems is the WV at the altitude
    2/3 of the cruising altitude.
2.  **061.01.08.02.02** -- WV used for descent problems is the WV at the
    altitude 1/2 of the descent altitude.
3.  **061.01.08.02.03** -- Calculate the average climb/descent GS from given TAS
    at various altitudes, and WV at various altitudes and true track.
