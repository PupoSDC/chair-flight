    turbine engine.

    basic, straight jet engine and perform simple calculations (including
    pressure thrust).

    approximately constant over the whole aeroplane subsonic speed range.

$$
T = m ( V\_{outlet} - V\_{inlet}) + A (P\_{inlet} - P\_{outlet})
$$
