# Dynamic longitudinal stability

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.04.03.16.01** -- Describe the phugoid and short-period motion in terms
    of period, damping, variations (if applicable) in speed, altitude, and
    alpha.
2.  **081.04.03.16.02** -- Explain why the short-period motion is more hazardous
    than the phugoid.
3.  **081.04.03.16.03** -- Describe "pilot-induced oscillations".
4.  **081.04.03.16.04** -- Explain the effect of high altitude on dynamic
    stability.
5.  **081.04.03.16.05** -- Describe the influence of the CG location on the
    dynamic longitudinal stability of the aeroplane.
