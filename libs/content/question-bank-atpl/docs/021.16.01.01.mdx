# Different types, design, operation, limitations

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.16.01.01.01** -- Describe the following main principles of helicopter
    transmission systems for single- and twin-engine helicopters: drive for the
    main and tail rotor; accessory drive for the generator(s), alternator(s),
    hydraulic and oil pumps, oil cooler(s) and tachometers.
2.  **021.16.01.01.02** -- Describe the reason for limitations on multi-engine
    helicopter transmissions in various engine-out situations.
3.  **021.16.01.01.03** -- Describe how the passive vibration control works with
    gearbox mountings.
