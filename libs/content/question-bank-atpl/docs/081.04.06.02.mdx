# Tendency to spiral dive

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.04.06.02.01** -- Explain how lateral and directional stability are
    coupled.
2.  **081.04.06.02.02** -- Explain how high static directional stability and low
    static lateral stability may cause spiral divergence (unstable spiral dive),
    and under which conditions the spiral dive mode is neutral or stable.
3.  **081.04.06.02.03** -- Describe an unstable spiral dive mode with respect to
    deviations in speed, bank angle, nose low-pitch attitude, and decreasing
    altitude.
