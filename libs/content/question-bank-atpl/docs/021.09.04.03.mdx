# AC distribution

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.09.04.03.01** -- Explain the difference in the principle of operation
    for a split AC electrical system and a parallel AC electrical system.
2.  **021.09.04.03.02** -- Describe the following distribution consequences:
    power transfer between different power supplies; power transfer in the event
    of a supply failure; loss of all normal AC supplies.
3.  **021.09.04.03.03** -- Give examples of AC consumers.
4.  **021.09.04.03.04** -- Explain the conditions to be met for paralleling AC
    generators.
5.  **021.09.04.03.05** -- State that volt-ampere (VA) is the unit for total
    power consumed in an AC system.
