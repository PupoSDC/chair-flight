# Perception

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.03.01.02.01** -- Name the basis of the perceptual process.
2.  **040.03.01.02.02** -- Describe the mechanism of perception
    ("bottom-up"/"top-down" process).
3.  **040.03.01.02.03** -- Illustrate why perception is subjective and state the
    relevant factors that influence interpretation of perceived information.
4.  **040.03.01.02.04** -- Describe some basic perceptual illusions.
5.  **040.03.01.02.05** -- Illustrate some basic perceptual concepts.
6.  **040.03.01.02.06** -- Give examples where perception plays a decisive role
    in flight safety.
7.  **040.03.01.02.07** -- Stress how persuasive and believable mistaken
    perception can manifest itself both for an individual and a group.
