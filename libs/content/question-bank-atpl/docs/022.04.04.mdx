# Directional gyroscope

## Learning Objectives

1.  **022.04.04.01.01** -- Explain the purpose of the directional gyroscope.
2.  **022.04.04.01.02** -- Identify the two types of gyro-driven direction
    indicators: direction indicator; horizontal situation indicator (HSI).
3.  **022.04.04.01.03** -- Explain how the directional gyroscope will drift over
    time due to the following: rotation of the Earth; aircraft manoeuvring;
    aircraft movement over the Earth"s surface/direction of travel.
4.  **022.04.04.01.04** -- Describe the procedure for the pilot to align the
    directional gyroscope to the correct compass heading.
