# Helicopter: air intake

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.11.02.06.01** -- Name and explain the main task of the engine air
    intake.
2.  **021.11.02.06.02** -- Describe the use of a convergent air-intake ducting
    on helicopters.
3.  **021.11.02.06.03** -- Describe the reasons for and the dangers of the
    following operational problems concerning engine air intake: airflow
    separations; intake icing; intake damage; FOD; heavy in-flight turbulence.
4.  **021.11.02.06.04** -- Describe the conditions and circumstances during
    ground operations when FOD is most likely to occur.
5.  **021.11.02.06.05** -- Describe and explain the principles of air intake
    filter systems that can be fitted to some helicopters for operations in
    icing and sand conditions.
6.  **021.11.02.06.06** -- Describe the function of the heated pads on some
    helicopter air intakes.
