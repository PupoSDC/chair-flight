# Constant speed drive (CSD) and integrated drive generator (IDG) systems

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.09.03.03.01** -- Describe the function of a CSD.
2.  **021.09.03.03.02** -- Explain the parameters of a CSD that are monitored.
3.  **021.09.03.03.03** -- Describe the function of an IDG.
4.  **021.09.03.03.04** -- Explain the consequences of a mechanical
    disconnection during flight for a CSD and an IDG.
5.  **021.09.03.03.05** -- Explain that a CSD/IDG has its own, independent oil
    system and how a leak from this may appear as an engine oil leak.
