# Properties

## Learning Objectives

1.  **061.03.01.01.01** -- Describe the geometric properties of a great circle
    (including the vertex) and a small circle.
2.  **061.03.01.01.02** -- Describe the geometric properties of a great circle
    and a small circle, up to 30 degrees difference of longitude.
3.  **061.03.01.01.03** -- Explain why a great-circle route is the shortest
    distance between any two positions on the Earth.
4.  **061.03.01.01.04** -- Name examples of great circles on the surface of the
    Earth.
