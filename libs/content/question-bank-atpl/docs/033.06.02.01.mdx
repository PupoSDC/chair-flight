# Deviation from planned data

## Learning Objectives

1.  **033.06.02.01.01** -- State that the commander is responsible for ensuring
    that, even in case of diversion, the remaining fuel is not less than the
    fuel required to proceed to an aerodrome where a safe landing can be made,
    with final reserve fuel remaining.
2.  **033.06.02.01.02** -- Explain that, in the case of an in-flight update, the
    commander has to check the following: the suitability of the new destination
    or alternate aerodrome; meteorological conditions on revised routing and at
    revised destination or alternate aerodrome; the aircraft must be able to
    land with the prescribed final reserve fuel.
3.  **033.06.02.01.03** -- Calculate the revised destination/alternate aerodrome
    landing mass from given latest data.
