# Safety culture and safety management

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.01.04.01.01** -- Distinguish between "open cultures" and "closed
    cultures".
2.  **040.01.04.01.02** -- Illustrate how safety culture is reflected in
    national culture.
3.  **040.01.04.01.03** -- Discuss the established expression "safety first" in
    a commercial entity.
4.  **040.01.04.01.04** -- Explain James Reason"s "Swiss Cheese Model".
5.  **040.01.04.01.05** -- State the important factors that promote a good
    safety culture.
6.  **040.01.04.01.06** -- Distinguish between "just culture" and "non-punitive
    culture".
7.  **040.01.04.01.07** -- Name the five components which form safety culture
    (according to James Reason: informed culture, reporting culture, learning
    culture, just culture, flexible culture).
8.  **040.01.04.01.08** -- Name the basic concepts of safety management system
    (SMS) (including hazard identification and risk management) and its
    relationship with safety culture in order to: define how the organisation is
    set up to manage risks; identify workplace risk and implement suitable
    controls; implement effective communication across all levels of the
    organisation.
