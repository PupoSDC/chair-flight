# Take-off

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.03.03.01.01** -- Determine the field-length-limited take-off mass and
    take-off speeds given defactored distance, configuration, pressure altitude,
    temperature and headwind/tailwind component.
2.  **032.03.03.01.02** -- Determine the accelerate-go distance and
    accelerate-stop distance data.
3.  **032.03.03.01.03** -- Determine the ground-roll distance and take-off
    distance from graphs.
4.  **032.03.03.01.04** -- Determine the all-engine-out and critical-engine-out
    take-off climb data.
5.  **032.03.03.01.05** -- Determine take off flight path for a MEP aeroplane of
    given mass and given airfield conditions, and calculate the obstacle
    clearance based on the take off flight path.
6.  **032.03.03.01.06** -- Determine the minimum headwind or maximum tailwind
    component required for take-off for a given mass and given airfield
    conditions.
7.  **032.03.03.01.07** -- Given take-off run available (TORA), TODA and ASDA,
    slope and surface conditions, calculate the defactored distance to be used
    for commercial air transport using the appropriate take-off graphs.
8.  **032.03.03.01.08** -- Calculate the minimum TORA or TODA for commercial air
    transport given the defactored take-off distance or run, runway surface and
    slope.
