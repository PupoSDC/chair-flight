# Reduction of visibility caused by other phenomena

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.09.09.02.01** -- Describe the reduction of visibility caused by low
    drifting and blowing snow.
2.  **050.09.09.02.02** -- Describe the reduction of visibility caused by low
    drifting and blowing dust and sand.
3.  **050.09.09.02.03** -- Describe the reduction of visibility caused by dust
    storm (DS) and sandstorm (SS).
4.  **050.09.09.02.04** -- Describe the reduction of visibility caused by icing
    (windshield).
5.  **050.09.09.02.05** -- Describe the reduction of visibility caused by the
    position of the sun relative to the visual direction.
6.  **050.09.09.02.06** -- Describe the reduction of visibility caused by the
    reflection of the sun"s rays from the top of the layers of haze, fog and
    clouds.
