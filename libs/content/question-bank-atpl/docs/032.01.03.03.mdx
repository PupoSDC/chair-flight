# Maximum endurance

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.01.03.03.01** -- Explain fuel flow in relation to TAS and thrust for a
    turbojet aeroplane.
2.  **032.01.03.03.02** -- State the speed for maximum endurance for a turbojet
    aeroplane.
3.  **032.01.03.03.03** -- Explain fuel flow in relation to TAS and thrust for a
    propeller-driven aeroplane.
4.  **032.01.03.03.04** -- State the speed for maximum endurance for a
    propeller-driven aeroplane and the disadvantages of holding at this speed
    (e.g. high angle of attack (AoA) and lack of speed stability).
5.  **032.01.03.03.05** -- Explain the effect of wind and altitude on endurance,
    and the maximum endurance speed for a turbojet aeroplane.
6.  **032.01.03.03.06** -- Explain the effect of wind and altitude on endurance,
    and the maximum endurance speed for a propeller-driven aeroplane.
7.  **032.01.03.03.07** -- Describe the benefits of managing your en-route
    airspeed to reduce or avoid holding time, and the operational situations
    when it could be used (commanded by the pilot or air traffic control (ATC),
    when delays at arrival airport occur).
