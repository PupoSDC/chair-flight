# Definitions and basic concepts of air

## Learning Objectives

1.  **082.01.01.02.01** -- Describe air temperature and pressure as functions of
    height.
2.  **082.01.01.02.02** -- Define the International Standard Atmosphere (ISA).
3.  **082.01.01.02.03** -- Define air density, and explain the relationship
    between air density, pressure, and temperature.
4.  **082.01.01.02.04** -- Explain the influence of moisture content on air
    density.
5.  **082.01.01.02.05** -- Define pressure altitude and air density altitude.
