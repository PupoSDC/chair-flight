# Cooperation

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.03.04.03.01** -- Distinguish between cooperation and coaction.
2.  **040.03.04.03.02** -- Define the term "group".
3.  **040.03.04.03.03** -- Illustrate the influence of interdependence in a
    group.
4.  **040.03.04.03.04** -- List the advantages and disadvantages of teamwork.
5.  **040.03.04.03.05** -- Explain the term "synergy".
6.  **040.03.04.03.06** -- Define the term "cohesion".
7.  **040.03.04.03.07** -- Define the term "groupthink".
8.  **040.03.04.03.08** -- State the essential conditions for good teamwork.
9.  **040.03.04.03.09** -- Explain the function of role and norm in a group.
10. **040.03.04.03.10** -- Name the different role patterns which occur in a
    group situation.
11. **040.03.04.03.11** -- Explain how behaviour can be affected by the
    following factors: persuasion; conformity; compliance; obedience.
12. **040.03.04.03.12** -- Distinguish between status and role.
13. **040.03.04.03.13** -- Stress the inherent dangers of a situation where
    there is a mix of role and status within the flight crew compartment.
14. **040.03.04.03.14** -- Explain the terms "leadership" and "followership".
15. **040.03.04.03.15** -- Describe the trans-cockpit authority gradient and its
    affiliated leadership styles (i.e. autocratic, laissez-faire and
    synergistic).
16. **040.03.04.03.16** -- Name the most important attributes of a positive
    leadership style.
