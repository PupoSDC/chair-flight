# Signs

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.09.04.04.01** -- Explain which signs are the only ones on the movement
    area utilising red.
2.  **010.09.04.04.02** -- List the provisions for illuminating signs.
3.  **010.09.04.04.03** -- Name the kinds of signs which shall be included in
    mandatory instruction signs.
4.  **010.09.04.04.04** -- Name the colours used for mandatory instruction
    signs.
5.  **010.09.04.04.05** -- Describe by which sign a pattern "A" RWY holding
    position (i.e. at an intersection of a TWY and a non-instrument,
    non-precision approach or take-off RWY) marking shall be supplemented.
6.  **010.09.04.04.06** -- Describe by which sign a pattern "B" RWY holding
    position (i.e. at an intersection of a TWY and a precision approach RWY)
    marking shall be supplemented.
7.  **010.09.04.04.07** -- Describe the location of: a RWY designation sign at a
    TWY/RWY intersection; a "NO ENTRY" sign; a RWY holding position sign.
8.  **010.09.04.04.08** -- State which sign indicates that a taxiing aircraft is
    about to infringe an obstacle limitation surface or interfere with the
    operation of radio navigation aids (e.g. ILS/MLS critical/sensitive area).
9.  **010.09.04.04.09** -- Describe the various possible inscriptions on RWY
    designation signs and on holding position signs.
10. **010.09.04.04.10** -- Describe the colours used in connection with
    information signs.
11. **010.09.04.04.11** -- Describe the possible inscriptions on information
    signs.
12. **010.09.04.04.12** -- Explain the application, location and characteristics
    of aircraft stand identification signs.
13. **010.09.04.04.13** -- Explain the application, location and characteristics
    of road holding position signs.
