# Airspace

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.07.01.03.01** -- Describe the purpose for establishing flight
    information regions (FIRs) including upper flight information regions
    (UIRs).
2.  **010.07.01.03.02** -- Describe the various rules and services that apply to
    the various classes of airspace.
3.  **010.07.01.03.03** -- Explain which airspace shall be included in an FIR or
    UIR.
4.  **010.07.01.03.04** -- State the designation for those portions of the
    airspace where flight information service (FIS) and alerting service shall
    be provided.
5.  **010.07.01.03.05** -- State the designations for those portions of the
    airspace where ATC services shall be provided.
6.  **010.07.01.03.06** -- Identify whether or not control areas (CTAs) and
    control zones (CTRs) designated within an FIR shall form part of that FIR.
7.  **010.07.01.03.07** -- State the lower limit of a CTA as far as ICAO
    Standards are concerned.
8.  **010.07.01.03.08** -- State whether or not the lower limit of a CTA has to
    be established uniformly.
9.  **010.07.01.03.09** -- Explain why a UIR or upper CTA should be delineated
    to include the upper airspace within the lateral limits of a number of lower
    FIRs or CTAs.
10. **010.07.01.03.10** -- Describe in general the lateral limits of CTRs.
11. **010.07.01.03.11** -- State the minimum extension (in NM) of the lateral
    limits of a CTR.
12. **010.07.01.03.12** -- State the upper limits of a CTR located within the
    lateral limits of a CTA.
