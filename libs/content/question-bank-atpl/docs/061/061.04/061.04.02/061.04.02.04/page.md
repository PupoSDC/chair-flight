  </Text>
  <Text variant="oneCorrect">
    The angle between the true great-circle track and the true rhumb-line track
    joining the following points: A (60º S 165º W) B (60º S 177º E), at the
    place of arrival B, is:
  </Text>
  <Option>15.6º</Option>
  <Option>9º</Option>
  <Option>5.2º</Option>
  <Option correct>7.8º</Option>

  <Explanation>
    $$ Convergency = dlong x sin mlat \= 18 x sin 60 \= 18 x 0.866 \= 15.6 $$
    The track will change a total of 15.6º during the entire flight. $$
    Conversion angle = 1/2 convergency \= 1/2 x 15.6 \= 7.8 $$
  </Explanation>
</Question>
