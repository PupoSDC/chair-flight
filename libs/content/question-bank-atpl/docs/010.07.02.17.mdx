# Radar services

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.07.02.17.01** -- State the basic identification procedures used with
    radar.
2.  **010.07.02.17.02** -- Define the term "PSR".
3.  **010.07.02.17.03** -- Describe the circumstances under which an aircraft
    provided with radar service should be informed of its position.
4.  **010.07.02.17.04** -- List the possible forms of position information
    passed on to the aircraft by radar services.
5.  **010.07.02.17.05** -- Describe the term "radar vectoring".
6.  **010.07.02.17.06** -- State the aims of radar vectoring as shown in ICAO
    Doc 4444.
7.  **010.07.02.17.07** -- Describe how radar vectoring shall be achieved.
8.  **010.07.02.17.08** -- Describe the information which shall be given to an
    aircraft when radar vectoring is terminated and the pilot is instructed to
    resume own navigation.
9.  **010.07.02.17.09** -- Explain the procedures for the conduct of
    surveillance radar approaches (SRAs).
10. **010.07.02.17.10** -- Describe what kind of action (concerning the
    transponder) the pilot is expected to perform in case of emergency if they
    have previously been directed by ATC to operate the transponder on a
    specific code.
