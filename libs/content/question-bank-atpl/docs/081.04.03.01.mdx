# Methods for achieving balance

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.04.03.01.01** -- Explain the stabiliser as the means to satisfy the
    condition of nullifying the total sum of the moments about the lateral axis.
2.  **081.04.03.01.02** -- Explain the influence of the location of the wing CP
    relative to the CG on the magnitude and direction of the balancing force on
    the stabiliser.
3.  **081.04.03.01.03** -- Explain the influence of the indicated airspeed on
    the magnitude and direction of the balancing force on the stabiliser.
4.  **081.04.03.01.04** -- Explain the use of the elevator deflection or
    stabiliser angle for the generation of the balancing force and its
    direction.
5.  **081.04.03.01.05** -- Explain the elevator deflection required to balance
    thrust change as a function of engine position.
