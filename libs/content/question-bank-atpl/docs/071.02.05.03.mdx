# Fire in the cabin, in the flight crew compartment and in the cargo compartment

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.02.05.03.01** -- Identify the different types of extinguishants used
    in handheld fire extinguishers and the type of fire for which each one may
    be used.
2.  **071.02.05.03.02** -- Describe the precautions to be considered when
    applying fire extinguishants.
3.  **071.02.05.03.03** -- Identify the appropriate handheld fire extinguishers
    to be used in the flight crew compartment, the passenger cabin and
    lavatories, and in the cargo compartments.
