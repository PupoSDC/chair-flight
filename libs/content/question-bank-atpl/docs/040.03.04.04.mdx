# Communication

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.03.04.04.01** -- Define the term "communication".
2.  **040.03.04.04.02** -- List the most basic components of interpersonal
    communication.
3.  **040.03.04.04.03** -- Explain the advantages of in-person two-way
    communication as opposed to one-way communication.
4.  **040.03.04.04.04** -- Intentionally left blank
5.  **040.03.04.04.05** -- Name the importance of non-verbal communication.
6.  **040.03.04.04.06** -- Describe the general aspects of non-verbal
    communication.
7.  **040.03.04.04.07** -- Describe the advantages/disadvantages of implicit and
    explicit communication.
8.  **040.03.04.04.08** -- Describe the advantages and possible problems of
    using "social" and "professional" language in high- and low-workload
    situations.
9.  **040.03.04.04.09** -- Name and explain the major obstacles to effective
    communication.
10. **040.03.04.04.10** -- Explain the difference between intrapersonal and
    interpersonal conflict.
11. **040.03.04.04.11** -- Describe the escalation process in human conflict.
12. **040.03.04.04.12** -- List the typical consequences of conflicts between
    crew members.
13. **040.03.04.04.13** -- Explain the following terms as part of the
    communication practice with regard to preventing or resolving conflicts:
    inquiry; active listening; advocacy; feedback; metacommunication;
    negotiation.
14. **040.03.04.04.14** -- Describe the limitations of communication in
    situations of high workload in the flight crew compartment in view of
    listening, verbal, non-verbal and visual effects.
