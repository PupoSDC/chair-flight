# Instruments and equipment

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.01.02.06.01** -- Explain which items do not require an equipment
    approval.
2.  **071.01.02.06.02** -- Explain the requirements regarding availability of
    spare electrical fuses.
3.  **071.01.02.06.03** -- Explain the requirements regarding windshield wipers.
4.  **071.01.02.06.04** -- List the minimum equipment required for day and night
    VFR flights.
5.  **071.01.02.06.05** -- List the minimum equipment required for IFR flights.
6.  **071.01.02.06.06** -- Explain the required additional equipment for
    single-pilot operations under IFR.
7.  **071.01.02.06.07** -- State the requirements for an altitude alerting
    system.
8.  **071.01.02.06.08** -- State the requirements for radio altimeters.
9.  **071.01.02.06.09** -- State the requirements for ground proximity warning
    system (GPWS)/terrain awareness and warning system (TAWS).
10. **071.01.02.06.10** -- State the requirements for airborne collision
    avoidance system (ACAS).
11. **071.01.02.06.11** -- State the conditions under which an aircraft must be
    fitted with a weather radar.
12. **071.01.02.06.12** -- State the circumstances under which a cockpit voice
    recorder (CVR) is compulsory (after 1998..
13. **071.01.02.06.13** -- State the rules regarding the location, construction,
    installation, and operation of cockpit voice recorders (CVRs) (after 1998..
14. **071.01.02.06.14** -- State the circumstances under which a flight data
    recorder (FDR) is compulsory (after 1998..
15. **071.01.02.06.15** -- State the rules regarding the location, construction,
    installation, and operation of flight data recorders (FDRs) (after 1998..
16. **071.01.02.06.16** -- Explain the requirements about seats, seat safety
    belts, harnesses, and child-restraint devices.
17. **071.01.02.06.17** -- Explain the requirements about "Fasten seat belt" and
    "No smoking" signs.
18. **071.01.02.06.18** -- Explain the requirements regarding internal doors and
    curtains.
19. **071.01.02.06.19** -- Explain the requirements regarding first-aid kits.
20. **071.01.02.06.20** -- Explain the requirements regarding emergency medical
    kits and first-aid oxygen.
21. **071.01.02.06.21** -- Detail the rules regarding crew protective breathing
    equipment.
22. **071.01.02.06.22** -- Describe the type and location of handheld fire
    extinguishers.
23. **071.01.02.06.23** -- Describe the location of crash axes and crowbars.
24. **071.01.02.06.24** -- Specify the colours and markings used to indicate
    break-in points.
25. **071.01.02.06.25** -- Explain the requirements for means of emergency
    evacuation.
26. **071.01.02.06.26** -- Explain the requirements for megaphones.
27. **071.01.02.06.27** -- Explain the requirements for emergency lighting and
    marking.
28. **071.01.02.06.28** -- Explain the requirements for an emergency locator
    transmitter (ELT).
29. **071.01.02.06.29** -- Explain the requirements for life jackets, life
    rafts, survival kits, and ELTs.
30. **071.01.02.06.30** -- Explain the requirements for crew survival suit.
31. **071.01.02.06.31** -- Explain the requirements for survival equipment.
32. **071.01.02.06.32** -- Explain the additional requirements for helicopters
    operating to or from helidecks located in hostile sea areas.
33. **071.01.02.06.33** -- Explain the requirements for emergency flotation
    equipment.
