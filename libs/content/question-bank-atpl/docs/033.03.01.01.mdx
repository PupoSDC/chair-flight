# Fuel planning (general)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **033.03.01.01.01** -- Convert to volume, mass and density given in
    different units which are commonly used in aviation.
2.  **033.03.01.01.02** -- Determine relevant data, such as fuel capacity, fuel
    flow/ consumption at different power/thrust settings, altitudes and
    atmospheric conditions, from the flight manual.
3.  **033.03.01.01.03** -- Calculate the attainable flight time/range from given
    average fuel flow/consumption and available amount of fuel.
4.  **033.03.01.01.04** -- Calculate the required fuel from given average fuel
    flow/ consumption and required time/range to be flown.
5.  **033.03.01.01.05** -- Calculate the required fuel for a VFR flight from
    given forecast meteorological conditions.
6.  **033.03.01.01.06** -- State the minimum amount of remaining fuel required
    on arrival at the destination and alternate aerodromes/ heliports.
7.  **033.03.01.01.07** -- Explain and describe how to calculate nautical air
    miles (NAM) from nautical ground miles (NGM).
8.  **033.03.01.01.08** -- Calculate the required fuel for an IFR flight from
    given forecast meteorological conditions.
