# Coverage and range

## Learning Objectives

1.  **062.02.05.03.01** -- Sketch the standard coverage area of the LOC and GP
    with angular sector limits in degrees and distance limits from the
    transmitter:

      distance of 25 NM from the runway, and 35 degrees on either side of the
      centre line to a distance of 17 NM from the runway;
    - GP coverage area is 8 degrees on either side of the centre line to a
      distance of minimum 10 NM from the runway.
