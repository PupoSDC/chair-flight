# Air traffic service (ATS) routes

## Learning Objectives

1.  **033.02.01.01.01** -- Identify suitable routings by identifying all
    relevant aeronautical and regulatory information (including information
    published in the national aeronautical information publication (AIP))
    required for IFR flight planning.
2.  **033.02.01.01.02** -- Identify and describe ATS routes (conventional, area
    navigation (RNAV), required navigation performance (RNP), conditional routes
    (CDRs), and direct routes).
