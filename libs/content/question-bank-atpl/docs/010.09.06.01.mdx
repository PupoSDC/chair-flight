# Visual aids for denoting restricted use of areas on RWYs and TWYs

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.09.06.01.01** -- Describe the colours and meaning of "closed markings"
    on RWYs and TWYs.
2.  **010.09.06.01.02** -- State how the pilot of an aircraft moving on the
    surface of a TWY, holding bay or apron shall be warned that the shoulders of
    these surfaces are "non-load-bearing".
3.  **010.09.06.01.03** -- Describe the pre-THR marking (including colours) when
    the surface before the THR is not suitable for normal use by aircraft.
