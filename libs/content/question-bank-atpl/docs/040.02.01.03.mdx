# High-altitude environment

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.02.01.03.01** -- State how an increase in altitude may change the
    proportion of ozone in the atmosphere and that aircraft can be equipped with
    special ozone removers.
2.  **040.02.01.03.02** -- State the sources of radiation at high altitude.
3.  **040.02.01.03.03** -- List the effects of excessive exposure to radiation.
4.  **040.02.01.03.04** -- List the factors that affect the relative humidity of
    both the atmosphere and cabin air.
5.  **040.02.01.03.05** -- List the effects of low humidity on human body to be
    spurious thirst, dry eyes, skin and mucous membranes, and indicate measures
    that can be taken: drinking water, using eye drops and aqueous creams.
