# Static electricity

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.09.01.01.01** -- Explain static electricity and describe the flying
    conditions where aircraft are most susceptible to build-up of static
    electricity.
2.  **021.09.01.01.02** -- Describe a static discharger and explain the
    following: its purpose; typical locations; pilot"s role of observing it
    during pre-flight inspection.
3.  **021.09.01.01.03** -- Explain why an aircraft must first be grounded before
    refuelling/defueling.
4.  **021.09.01.01.04** -- Explain the reason for electrical bonding.
