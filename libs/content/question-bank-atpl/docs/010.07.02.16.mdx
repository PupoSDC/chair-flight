# Procedures for aerodrome (AD) control service

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.07.02.16.01** -- Name the operational failure or irregularity of AD
    equipment which shall be reported by the TWR immediately.
2.  **010.07.02.16.02** -- Explain that, after a given period of time, the TWR
    shall report to the area control centre (ACC) or flight information centre
    (FIC) if an aircraft does not land as expected.
3.  **010.07.02.16.03** -- Describe the procedures to be observed by the TWR
    whenever VFR operations are suspended.
4.  **010.07.02.16.04** -- Explain the term "RWY-in-use" and its selection.
5.  **010.07.02.16.05** -- List the information the TWR should give to an
    aircraft prior to: taxiing for take-off; take-off; entering the traffic
    circuit.
6.  **010.07.02.16.06** -- Explain that a report of surface wind direction given
    to a pilot by the TWR is magnetic.
7.  **010.07.02.16.07** -- Explain the exact meaning of the expression "RWY
    vacated".
