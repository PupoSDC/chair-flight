# Missed approach

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.06.04.04.01** -- Name the three phases of a missed approach procedure
    and describe their geometric limits.
2.  **010.06.04.04.02** -- State the main task of a missed approach procedure.
3.  **010.06.04.04.03** -- Define the term "missed approach point (MAPt)".
4.  **010.06.04.04.04** -- Describe how an MAPt may be established in an
    approach procedure.
5.  **010.06.04.04.05** -- State the pilot"s action if, upon reaching the MAPt,
    the required visual reference is not established.
6.  **010.06.04.04.06** -- Describe what a pilot is expected to do in the event
    a missed approach is initiated prior to arriving at the MAPt.
7.  **010.06.04.04.07** -- State whether the pilot is obliged to cross the MAPt
    at the height (HGT)/altitude (ALT) required by the procedure or whether they
    are allowed to cross the MAPt at a HGT/ALT greater than that required by the
    procedure.
