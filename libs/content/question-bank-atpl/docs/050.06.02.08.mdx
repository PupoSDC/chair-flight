# Movement of fronts and pressure systems, life cycle

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.06.02.08.01** -- Describe the movements of fronts and pressure systems
    and the life cycle of a mid-latitude depression.
2.  **050.06.02.08.02** -- State the rules for predicting the direction and the
    speed of movement of fronts.
3.  **050.06.02.08.03** -- State the difference in the speed of movement between
    cold and warm fronts.
4.  **050.06.02.08.04** -- State the rules for predicting the direction and the
    speed of movement of frontal depressions.
5.  **050.06.02.08.05** -- Describe, with a sketch if required, the genesis,
    development and life cycle of a frontal depression with associated cloud and
    rain belts.
