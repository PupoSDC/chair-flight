# Theory and model of human error

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.03.02.03.01** -- Explain the concept of the "error chain".
2.  **040.03.02.03.02** -- Differentiate between an isolated error and an error
    chain.
3.  **040.03.02.03.03** -- Distinguish between the main forms/types of errors
    (i.e. slips, faults, omissions and violations).
4.  **040.03.02.03.04** -- Discuss the above errors and their relevance in
    flight.
5.  **040.03.02.03.05** -- Distinguish between an active and a latent error, and
    give examples.
