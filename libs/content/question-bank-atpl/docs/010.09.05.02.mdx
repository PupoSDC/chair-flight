# Lighting of objects

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.09.05.02.01** -- Name the different types of lights to indicate the
    presence of objects which must be lighted.
2.  **010.09.05.02.02** -- Describe (in general terms) the location of obstacle
    lights.
3.  **010.09.05.02.03** -- Describe (in general and for normal circumstances)
    the colour and sequence of low-intensity obstacle lights, medium-intensity
    obstacle lights and high-intensity obstacle lights.
4.  **010.09.05.02.04** -- State that information about lights to be displayed
    by aircraft is provided in both ICAO Annex 2 (Rules of the Air) and SERA.
