# The airflow around the fuselage

## Learning Objectives

1.  **082.01.03.04.01** -- Describe the fuselage and the external components
    that cause (parasite) drag, the airflow around the fuselage, and the
    influence of the pitch angle of the fuselage. Describe fuselage shapes that
    minimise drag.
2.  **082.01.03.04.02** -- Define profile drag as the sum of pressure (form)
    drag and skin friction drag.
3.  **082.01.03.04.03** -- Define "interference drag".
4.  **082.01.03.04.04** -- Know the drag formula.
