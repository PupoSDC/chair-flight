# Occlusions, associated clouds and weather

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.06.02.06.01** -- Define the term "occlusion" and "occluded front".
2.  **050.06.02.06.02** -- Describe the cloud, weather, ground visibility and
    aviation hazards in a cold occlusion.
3.  **050.06.02.06.03** -- Describe the cloud, weather, ground visibility and
    aviation hazards in a warm occlusion.
4.  **050.06.02.06.04** -- Explain the seasonal differences in the weather at
    occlusions.
5.  **050.06.02.06.05** -- Sketch a cross section of occlusions showing weather,
    cloud and aviation hazards.
6.  **050.06.02.06.06** -- On a sketch illustrate the development of an
    occlusion and the movement of the occlusion point.
