# Manoeuvring-load diagram

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.06.02.01.01** -- Describe the manoeuvring-load diagram.
2.  **081.06.02.01.02** -- Define limit and ultimate load factor, and explain
    what can happen if these values are exceeded.
3.  **081.06.02.01.03** -- Define "VA", "VB", "VC", and "VD".
4.  **081.06.02.01.04** -- Identify and explain the varying features on the VN
    diagram: load factor "n"; speed scale, equivalent airspeed; equivalent
    airspeed envelope; 1g stall speed; stall boundary (refer to 081 03 01 02..
5.  **081.06.02.01.05** -- Describe the relationship between VMO or VNE and VC.
6.  **081.06.02.01.06** -- State all the manoeuvring load-factors limits
    applicable to CS-23 and CS-25 aeroplanes.
7.  **081.06.02.01.07** -- Explain the relationship between VA and VS in a
    formula, and calculate the values.
8.  **081.06.02.01.08** -- Explain the significance of VA and the adverse
    consequences of applying full, abrupt nose-up elevator deflection when
    exceeding VA.
