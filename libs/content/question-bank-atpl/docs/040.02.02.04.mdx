# Hearing

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.02.02.04.01** -- State the basic parts and functions of the outer, the
    middle and the inner ear.
2.  **040.02.02.04.02** -- Differentiate between the functions of the vestibular
    apparatus and the cochlea in the inner ear.
3.  **040.02.02.04.03** -- Define the main causes of the following hearing
    defects/loss: "conductive deafness"; "noise-induced hearing loss" (NIHL);
    "presbycusis".
4.  **040.02.02.04.04** -- Summarise the effects of environmental noise on
    hearing.
5.  **040.02.02.04.05** -- State the decibel level of received noise that will
    cause NIHL.
6.  **040.02.02.04.06** -- Identify the potential occupational risks that may
    cause hearing loss.
7.  **040.02.02.04.07** -- List the main sources of hearing loss in the flying
    environment.
8.  **040.02.02.04.08** -- List the precautions that may be taken to reduce the
    probability of onset of hearing loss.
