# Forces on the blade in forward flight without cyclic feathering

## Learning Objectives

1.  **082.05.02.01.01** -- Assume rigid attachments of the blade to the hub and
    show the periodic lift, moment and stresses on the attachment, the ensuing
    metal fatigue, the roll moment on the helicopter, and justify the necessity
    for a flapping hinge.
2.  **082.05.02.01.02** -- Assume no cyclic pitch and describe the lift on the
    advancing and retreating blades.
3.  **082.05.02.01.03** -- State the azimuthal phase lag (90 degrees or less)
    between the input (applied pitch) and the output (flapping angle). Explain
    flapback (the rearward tilting of the tip path plane and total rotor
    thrust).
