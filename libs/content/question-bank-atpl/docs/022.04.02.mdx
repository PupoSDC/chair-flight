# Rate-of-turn indicator - Turn coordinator - Balance (slip) indicator

## Learning Objectives

1.  **022.04.02.01.01** -- Explain the purpose of a rate-of-turn and balance
    (slip) indicator.
2.  **022.04.02.01.02** -- Define a "rate-1 turn".
3.  **022.04.02.01.03** -- Describe the indications given by a rate-of-turn
    indicator.
4.  **022.04.02.01.04** -- Explain the relation between bank angle, rate of turn
    and TAS, and how bank angle becomes the limiting factor at high speed (no
    calculations).
5.  **022.04.02.01.05** -- Explain the purpose of a balance (slip) indicator and
    its principle of operation.
6.  **022.04.02.01.06** -- Describe the indications of a rate-of-turn and
    balance (slip) indicator during a balanced, slip or skid turn.
7.  **022.04.02.01.07** -- Describe the indications given by a turn coordinator
    (or turn-and-bank indicator).
8.  **022.04.02.01.08** -- Compare the indications on the rate-of-turn indicator
    and the turn coordinator.
