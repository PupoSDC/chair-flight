# Reserve fuel and its components

## Learning Objectives

1.  **033.03.02.03.01** -- Explain the reasons for having contingency fuel.
2.  **033.03.02.03.02** -- Calculate the contingency fuel according to the
    applicable operational requirements.
3.  **033.03.02.03.03** -- Explain the reasons and regulations for having
    alternate fuel and name the segments of flight for which the alternate fuel
    is relevant.
4.  **033.03.02.03.04** -- Calculate the alternate fuel in accordance with the
    applicable operational requirements and relevant data from the navigation
    plan and the flight manual.
5.  **033.03.02.03.05** -- Explain the reasons and regulations for having final
    reserve fuel.
6.  **033.03.02.03.06** -- Calculate the final reserve fuel for an aircraft in
    accordance with the applicable operational requirements and by using
    relevant data from the flight manual.
7.  **033.03.02.03.07** -- Explain the reasons and regulations for having
    additional fuel.
8.  **033.03.02.03.08** -- Calculate the additional fuel for a flight in
    accordance with the applicable operational requirements.
