# Hydraulic fluids: types, characteristics, limitations

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.03.02.01.01** -- List and explain the desirable properties of a
    hydraulic fluid with regard to:
    - thermal stability;
    - corrosiveness;
    - flashpoint and flammability;
    - volatility;
    - viscosity.
2.  **021.03.02.01.02** -- State that hydraulic fluids are irritating to skin
    and eyes.
3.  **021.03.02.01.03** -- List the two different types of hydraulic fluids:
    - synthetic;
    - mineral.
4.  **021.03.02.01.04** -- State that different types of hydraulic fluids cannot
    be mixed.
5.  **021.03.02.01.05** -- State that at the pressures being considered,
    hydraulic fluid is considered incompressible.

## Summary

### Properties of Hydraulic fluids

- Thermally stable;
- Non-corrosiveness;
- High flashpoint;
- Low flammability;
- low volatility;
- High viscosity;
