# Stabiliser trim

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.05.08.03.01** -- Describe the working principle of a stabiliser trim
    including the flight deck indications.
2.  **081.05.08.03.02** -- Explain the advantages and disadvantages of a
    stabiliser trim compared to a trim tab.
3.  **081.05.08.03.03** -- Explain the relationship between CG position,
    take-off trim setting, and stabiliser trim position.
4.  **081.05.08.03.04** -- Explain the effect of errors in the take-off
    stabiliser trim setting on the rotation characteristics and stick force
    during take-off rotation.
5.  **081.05.08.03.05** -- Discuss the effects of jammed and runaway stabiliser.
6.  **081.05.08.03.06** -- Explain the consequences of a jammed stabiliser
    during take-off, landing, and go-around.
