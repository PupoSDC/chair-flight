# Regulation (EU) No 965/2012 - Annex IV (Part-CAT) and Annex V (Part-SPA)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.02.12.03.01** -- Explain the terminology relevant to dangerous goods.
2.  **071.02.12.03.02** -- Explain the scope of that Regulation.
3.  **071.02.12.03.03** -- Explain why the transport of dangerous goods by air
    is subject to operator approval.
4.  **071.02.12.03.04** -- Explain the limitations on the transport of dangerous
    goods by air.
5.  **071.02.12.03.05** -- Explain the requirements for the acceptance of
    dangerous goods.
6.  **071.02.12.03.06** -- Explain the requirements regarding inspection for
    damage, leakage or contamination.
7.  **071.02.12.03.07** -- Explain the requirement for the provision of
    information to flight crew.
8.  **071.02.12.03.08** -- Explain the requirements for dangerous goods incident
    and accident reports.
9.  **071.02.12.03.09** -- State that some articles and substances, which would
    otherwise be classed as dangerous goods, can be exempted if they are part of
    the aircraft equipment, or required for use during aeromedical flights.
10. **071.02.12.03.10** -- Explain why some articles and substances may be
    forbidden for transport by air.
11. **071.02.12.03.11** -- Explain why packing must comply with the
    specifications of the Technical Instructions.
12. **071.02.12.03.12** -- Explain the need for an inspection prior to loading
    dangerous goods on an aircraft.
13. **071.02.12.03.13** -- Explain why some dangerous goods are designated for
    carriage only on cargo aircraft.
14. **071.02.12.03.14** -- Explain how misdeclared or undeclared dangerous goods
    found in baggage are to be reported.
