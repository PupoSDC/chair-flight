# Coordination (multi-crew concepts)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.03.04.02.01** -- Name the objectives of the multi-crew concept.
2.  **040.03.04.02.02** -- State and explain the elements of multi-crew
    concepts.
3.  **040.03.04.02.03** -- Describe the concepts of "standard operating
    procedures" (SOPs), checklists and crew briefings.
4.  **040.03.04.02.04** -- Describe the purpose of and procedure for crew
    briefings.
5.  **040.03.04.02.05** -- Describe the purpose of and procedure for checklists.
6.  **040.03.04.02.06** -- Describe the function of communication in a
    coordinated team.
7.  **040.03.04.02.07** -- Explain the advantages of SOPs.
8.  **040.03.04.02.08** -- Explain how SOPs contribute to avoiding, reducing and
    managing threats and errors.
9.  **040.03.04.02.09** -- Explain potential threats of SOPs, for example during
    company or type conversion (e.g. motor programmes, company culture,
    hazardous attitudes, developed habits).
