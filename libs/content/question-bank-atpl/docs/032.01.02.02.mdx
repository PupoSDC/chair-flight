# Definitions and terms

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.01.02.02.01** -- Define the terms "climb angle" and "climb gradient".
2.  **032.01.02.02.02** -- Define the terms "flight-path angle" and "flight-path
    gradient".
3.  **032.01.02.02.03** -- Define the terms "descent angle" and "descent
    gradient".
4.  **032.01.02.02.04** -- Explain the difference between climb/descent angle
    and flight-path angle.
5.  **032.01.02.02.05** -- Define "absolute ceiling".
6.  **032.01.02.02.06** -- Describe "clearway" and "stopway" according to
    CS-Definitions.
7.  **032.01.02.02.07** -- Describe: take-off run available (TORA); take-off
    distance available (TODA); accelerate-stop distance available (ASDA); and
    determine each from given data or appropriate aerodrome charts.
8.  **032.01.02.02.08** -- Describe "screen height" including its various
    values.
9.  **032.01.02.02.09** -- Define the terms "range" and "endurance".
10. **032.01.02.02.10** -- Define an aeroplane"s "specific range" (SR) in terms
    of nautical air miles (NAM) per unit of fuel, and "specific range over the
    ground" (SRG) in terms of nautical ground miles (NGM) per unit of fuel.
11. **032.01.02.02.11** -- Define the power available and power required.
