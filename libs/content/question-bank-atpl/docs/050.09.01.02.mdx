# Types of ice accretion

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.09.01.02.01** -- Define "clear ice".
2.  **050.09.01.02.02** -- Describe the conditions for the formation of clear
    ice.
3.  **050.09.01.02.03** -- Explain the formation of the structure of clear ice
    with the release of latent heat during the freezing process.
4.  **050.09.01.02.04** -- Describe the aspects of clear ice: appearance,
    weight, solidity.
5.  **050.09.01.02.05** -- Define "rime ice".
6.  **050.09.01.02.06** -- Describe the conditions for the formation of rime
    ice.
7.  **050.09.01.02.07** -- Describe the aspects of rime ice: appearance, weight,
    solidity.
8.  **050.09.01.02.08** -- Define "mixed ice".
9.  **050.09.01.02.09** -- Describe the conditions for the formation of mixed
    ice.
10. **050.09.01.02.10** -- Describe the aspects of mixed ice: appearance,
    weight, solidity.
11. **050.09.01.02.11** -- Describe the possible process of ice formation in
    snow conditions.
12. **050.09.01.02.12** -- Define "hoar frost".
13. **050.09.01.02.13** -- Describe the conditions for the formation of hoar
    frost.
14. **050.09.01.02.14** -- Describe the aspects of hoar frost: appearance,
    solidity.
