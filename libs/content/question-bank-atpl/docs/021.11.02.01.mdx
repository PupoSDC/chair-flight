# Aeroplane: air intake

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.11.02.01.01** -- State the functions of the engine air inlet/air
    intake.
2.  **021.11.02.01.02** -- Describe the geometry of a subsonic (pitot-type) air
    inlet.
3.  **021.11.02.01.03** -- Explain the gas-parameter changes in a subsonic air
    inlet at different flight speeds.
4.  **021.11.02.01.04** -- Describe the reasons for, and the dangers of, the
    following operational problems concerning the engine air inlet:
    - airflow separation;
    - inlet icing;
    - inlet damage;
    - foreign object damage (FOD);
    - heavy in-flight turbulence.
