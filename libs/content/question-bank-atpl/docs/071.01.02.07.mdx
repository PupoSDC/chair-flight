# Communication and navigation equipment

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.01.02.07.01** -- Explain the general requirements for communication
    and navigation equipment.
2.  **071.01.02.07.02** -- Explain why the radio-communication equipment must be
    able to send and receive on 121.5 MHz.
3.  **071.01.02.07.03** -- Explain the requirements regarding the provision of
    an audio selector panel.
4.  **071.01.02.07.04** -- List the requirements for radio equipment when flying
    under VFR by reference to visual landmarks.
5.  **071.01.02.07.05** -- List the requirements for communication and
    navigation equipment when operating under IFR or under VFR over routes not
    navigated by reference to visual landmarks.
6.  **071.01.02.07.06** -- Explain what equipment is required to operate in
    airspace with reduced vertical separation minima (RVSM).
7.  **071.01.02.07.07** -- Explain the conditions under which a crew member
    interphone system and public address system are mandatory.
8.  **071.01.02.07.08** -- List the equipment for operations requiring a radio
    communication.
9.  **071.01.02.07.09** -- List the equipment for operations that require a
    radio navigation system.
10. **071.01.02.07.10** -- Explain the requirements regarding the provision of a
    transponder.
11. **071.01.02.07.11** -- Explain the requirements regarding the management of
    aeronautical databases.
