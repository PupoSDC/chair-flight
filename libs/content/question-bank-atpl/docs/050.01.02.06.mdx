# Temperature near the Earth"s surface, insolation, surface effects, effect of clouds, effect of wind

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.01.02.06.01** -- Explain the cooling/warming of the surface of the
    Earth by radiation.
2.  **050.01.02.06.02** -- Explain the cooling/warming of the air by molecular
    or turbulent heat transfer to/from the earth or sea surfaces.
3.  **050.01.02.06.03** -- Describe qualitatively the influence of the clouds on
    the cooling and warming of the surface and the air near the surface.
4.  **050.01.02.06.04** -- Explain the influence of the wind on the cooling and
    warming of the air near the surfaces.
