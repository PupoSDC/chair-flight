# Straight, steady descent

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.08.01.03.01** -- Describe the forces that act on an aeroplane in a
    straight, steady descent.
2.  **081.08.01.03.02** -- Name the forces parallel and perpendicular to the
    direction of flight. Apply the formula for forces parallel to the direction
    of flight (T equals D - W sin gamma). Apply the formula relating to the
    perpendicular forces (L equals W cos gamma).
3.  **081.08.01.03.03** -- Explain why lift is less than weight.
4.  **081.08.01.03.04** -- Explain why thrust is less than drag.
