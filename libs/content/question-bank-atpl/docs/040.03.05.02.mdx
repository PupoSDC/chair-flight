# Individual differences in personality and motivation

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.03.05.02.01** -- Describe the individual differences in personality by
    means of a common trait model (e.g. Eysenck"s personality factors) and use
    it to describe today"s ideal pilot.
2.  **040.03.05.02.02** -- Define the term "self-concept" and the role it plays
    in any change of personality.
3.  **040.03.05.02.03** -- Explain how a self-concept of under-confidence may
    lead to an outward show of aggression and self- assertiveness.
4.  **040.03.05.02.04** -- Define "self-discipline" and justify its importance
    for flight safety.
