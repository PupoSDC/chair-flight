# Presentation and interpretation

## Learning Objectives

1.  **062.02.03.02.01** -- Read off the radial on an RMI.
2.  **062.02.03.02.02** -- Read off the angular displacement in relation to a
    preselected radial on a horizontal situation indicator (HSI) or omnibearing
    indicator (OBI).
3.  **062.02.03.02.03** -- Explain the use of the TO/FROM indicator in order to
    determine aircraft position relative to the VOR considering also the heading
    of the aircraft.
4.  **062.02.03.02.04** -- Interpret VOR information as displayed on HSI, CDI
    and RMI.
5.  **062.02.03.02.05** -- Describe the following in-flight VOR procedures:
    tracking, and explain the influence of wind when tracking; interception of a
    radial inbound and outbound to/from a VOR; changing from one radial
    inbound/outbound to another; determining station passage and the abeam
    point.
6.  **062.02.03.02.06** -- State that when converting a radial into a true
    bearing, the variation at the VOR station has to be taken into account.
