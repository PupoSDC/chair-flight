# Departure, destination and alternate aerodromes

## Learning Objectives

1.  **033.04.01.02.01** -- Find and analyse the latest state at the departure,
    destination and alternate aerodromes, in particular for: opening hours; work
    in progress (WIP); special procedures due to WIP; obstructions; changes of
    frequencies for communications, navigation aids and facilities.
2.  **033.04.01.02.02** -- Check that satellite-based facilities are available
    during the expected time of use.
3.  **033.04.01.02.03** -- Check that GBAS/SBAS augmentation is available during
    the expected time of use.
