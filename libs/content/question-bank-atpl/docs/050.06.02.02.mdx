# Warm front, associated clouds and weather

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.06.02.02.01** -- Define a "warm front".
2.  **050.06.02.02.02** -- Describe the cloud, weather, ground visibility and
    aviation hazards at a warm front depending on the stability of the warm air.
3.  **050.06.02.02.03** -- Explain the seasonal differences in the weather at
    warm fronts.
4.  **050.06.02.02.04** -- Describe the structure, slope and dimensions of a
    warm front.
5.  **050.06.02.02.05** -- Sketch a cross section of a warm front showing
    weather, cloud and aviation hazards.
