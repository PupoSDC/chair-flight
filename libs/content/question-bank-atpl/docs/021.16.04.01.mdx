# Power, construction, materials, speed and torque

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.16.04.01.01** -- Describe how power is transmitted from the engine to
    the main-rotor gearbox.
2.  **021.16.04.01.02** -- Describe the material and construction of the
    driveshaft.
3.  **021.16.04.01.03** -- Explain the need for alignment between the engine and
    the main- rotor gearbox.
4.  **021.16.04.01.04** -- Identify how temporary misalignment occurs between
    driving and driven components.
5.  **021.16.04.01.05** -- Explain the relationship between driveshaft speed and
    torque.
6.  **021.16.04.01.06** -- Describe the methods with which power is delivered to
    the tail rotor.
7.  **021.16.04.01.07** -- Describe and identify the construction and materials
    of tail-rotor/Fenestron driveshafts.
