# Notices to airmen (NOTAMs)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.08.04.02.01** -- Describe how information shall be published which in
    principle would belong to NOTAMs but includes extensive text or graphics.
2.  **010.08.04.02.02** -- Summarise the essential information which leads to
    the issue of a NOTAM.
3.  **010.08.04.02.03** -- State how NOTAMs shall be distributed.
4.  **010.08.04.02.04** -- Explain how information regarding snow, ice and
    standing water on AD pavements shall be reported.
5.  **010.08.04.02.05** -- Describe the means by which NOTAMs shall be
    distributed.
6.  **010.08.04.02.06** -- Define and state which information an ASHTAM may
    contain.
