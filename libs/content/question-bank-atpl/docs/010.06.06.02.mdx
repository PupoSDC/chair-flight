# Procedures for operators and pilots

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.06.06.02.01** -- State on which setting at least one altimeter shall
    be set prior to take-off.
2.  **010.06.06.02.02** -- State where during the climb the altimeter setting
    shall be changed from QNH to 1013.2 hPa.
3.  **010.06.06.02.03** -- Describe when a pilot of an aircraft intending to
    land at an AD shall obtain the TRL.
4.  **010.06.06.02.04** -- Describe when a pilot of an aircraft intending to
    land at an AD shall obtain the actual QNH altimeter setting.
5.  **010.06.06.02.05** -- State where the altimeter settings shall be changed
    from 1013.2 hPa to QNH during descent for landing.
