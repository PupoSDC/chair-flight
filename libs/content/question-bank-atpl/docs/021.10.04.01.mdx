# Carburettor: design, operation, degraded modes of operation, indications and warnings

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.10.04.01.01** -- State the purpose of a carburettor.
2.  **021.10.04.01.02** -- Describe the operating principle of the simple float
    chamber carburettor.
3.  **021.10.04.01.03** -- Describe the methods of obtaining mixture control
    over the whole operating engine power setting range (compensation jet,
    diffuser).
4.  **021.10.04.01.04** -- Describe the methods of obtaining mixture control
    over the whole operating altitude range.
5.  **021.10.04.01.05** -- Explain the purpose and the operating principle of an
    accelerator pump.
6.  **021.10.04.01.06** -- Explain the purpose of power enrichment.
7.  **021.10.04.01.07** -- Describe the function of the carburettor heat system.
8.  **021.10.04.01.08** -- Explain the effect of carburettor heat on mixture
    ratio and power output.
9.  **021.10.04.01.09** -- Explain the purpose and the operating principle of a
    primer pump.
10. **021.10.04.01.10** -- Discuss other methods for priming an engine
    (acceleration pumps).
11. **021.10.04.01.11** -- Explain the danger of carburettor fire, including
    corrective measures.
