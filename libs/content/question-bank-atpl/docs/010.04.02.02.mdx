# Content and structure

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.04.02.02.01** -- Explain the structure of Part-FCL.
2.  **010.04.02.02.02** -- Explain the requirements to act as a flight crew
    member of a civil aircraft registered in a Member State, and know the
    general principles of the licensing system (light aircraft pilot licence
    (LAPL), private pilot licence (PPL), commercial pilot licence (CPL),
    multi-crew pilot licence (MPL), airline transport pilot licence (ATPL)).
3.  **010.04.02.02.03** -- List the two factors that are relevant to the
    exercise of the privileges of a licence.
4.  **010.04.02.02.04** -- State the circumstances in which a language
    proficiency endorsement is required.
5.  **010.04.02.02.05** -- List the restrictions for licence holders with an age
    of 60 years or more.
6.  **010.04.02.02.06** -- Explain the term "competent authority".
7.  **010.04.02.02.07** -- Describe the obligation to carry and present
    documents (e.g. a flight crew licence) under Part-FCL.
