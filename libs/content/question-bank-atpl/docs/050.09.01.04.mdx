# Ice crystal icing

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.09.01.04.01** -- Describe ice crystal icing.
2.  **050.09.01.04.02** -- Describe the atmospheric processes leading to high
    ice crystal concentration. Define the variable ice water content (IWC).
3.  **050.09.01.04.03** -- Identify weather situations and their relevant areas
    where high concentrations of ice crystals are likely to occur.
4.  **050.09.01.04.04** -- Name, in general, the flight hazards associated with
    high concentrations of ice crystals.
5.  **050.09.01.04.05** -- Explain how a pilot may possibly avoid areas with a
    high concentration of ice crystals.
