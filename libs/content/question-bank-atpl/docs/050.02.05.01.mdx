# Origin and characteristics

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.02.05.01.01** -- Explain the origin and formation of mountain waves.
2.  **050.02.05.01.02** -- State the conditions necessary for the formation of
    mountain waves.
3.  **050.02.05.01.03** -- Describe the structure and properties of mountain
    waves.
4.  **050.02.05.01.04** -- Explain how mountain waves may be identified by their
    associated meteorological phenomena.
5.  **050.02.05.01.05** -- Describe that mountain wave effects can exceed the
    performance or structural capability of aircraft.
6.  **050.02.05.01.06** -- Describe that mountain wave effects can propagate
    from low to high level, e.g. over Greenland and elsewhere.
