# Threat and error management (TEM) model and SHELL model

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.01.03.01.01** -- Explain the three components of the TEM model.
2.  **040.01.03.01.02** -- Explain and give examples of latent threats.
3.  **040.01.03.01.03** -- Explain and give examples of environmental threats.
4.  **040.01.03.01.04** -- Explain and give examples of organisational threats.
5.  **040.01.03.01.05** -- Explain and give a definition of "error" according to
    the TEM model of ICAO Doc 9683 (Part II, Chapter 2..
6.  **040.01.03.01.06** -- Give examples of different countermeasures which may
    be used in order to manage threats, errors, and undesired aircraft states.
7.  **040.01.03.01.07** -- Explain and give examples of procedural error,
    communication errors, and aircraft handling errors.
8.  **040.01.03.01.08** -- Explain and give examples of "undesired aircraft
    states".
9.  **040.01.03.01.09** -- State the components of the SHELL model.
10. **040.01.03.01.10** -- State the relevance of the SHELL model to the work in
    the cockpit.
