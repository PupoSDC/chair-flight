# Miscellaneous separation procedures

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.07.02.14.01** -- State the minimum separation between departing and
    arriving aircraft.
2.  **010.07.02.14.02** -- State the non-radar wake-turbulence longitudinal
    separation minima.
3.  **010.07.02.14.03** -- Describe the consequences of a clearance to "maintain
    own separation" while in VMC.
4.  **010.07.02.14.04** -- Give a brief description of "essential traffic" and
    "essential traffic information".
5.  **010.07.02.14.05** -- Describe the circumstances under which a reduction in
    separation minima may be allowed.
