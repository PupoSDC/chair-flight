# Private international law

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.01.02.05.01** -- Explain the legal significance of the issue of a
    passenger ticket or of baggage/cargo documents (that the issue is a form of
    contract).
2.  **010.01.02.05.02** -- Describe the consequences for an airline or the PIC
    when a document of carriage is not issued (that the contract is unaffected).
3.  **010.01.02.05.03** -- Explain the consequences for an airline operator of
    Regulation (EC) No 261/2004 on passenger rights in the event of delay,
    cancellation or denial of boarding.
4.  **010.01.02.05.04** -- Explain the liability limit in relation to
    destruction, loss, damage or delay of baggage.
