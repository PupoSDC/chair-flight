# Obstacle-limited take-off

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.04.01.09.01** -- Describe the operational regulations for obstacle
    clearance in the net take-off flight path (NTOFP).
2.  **032.04.01.09.02** -- Define the actual and NTOFP with
    one-engine-inoperative in accordance with CS-25.
3.  **032.04.01.09.03** -- Explain the effects of aeroplane configuration and
    meteorological conditions on the obstacle-limited take-off mass.
4.  **032.04.01.09.04** -- Describe the segments of the actual take-off flight
    path.
5.  **032.04.01.09.05** -- Describe the changes in the configuration, power,
    thrust and speed in the NTOFP climb segments.
6.  **032.04.01.09.06** -- State the standard maximum bank angle(s) in the first
    and second segment, and determine the effect on the stall speed and
    implication on V2.
7.  **032.04.01.09.07** -- Explain the influence of airspeed selection,
    acceleration and turns on the climb gradient.
8.  **032.04.01.09.08** -- Describe the European Union airworthiness
    requirements according to CS-25 relating to aeroplane performance take-off
    climb and flight path.
