# General aspects

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.04.02.01.01** -- Define "fog", "mist" and "haze" with reference to the
    WMO standards of visibility range.
2.  **050.04.02.01.02** -- Explain briefly the formation of fog, mist and haze.
3.  **050.04.02.01.03** -- Name the factors that generally contribute to the
    formation of fog and mist.
4.  **050.04.02.01.04** -- Name the factors that contribute to the formation of
    haze.
5.  **050.04.02.01.05** -- Describe freezing fog and ice fog.
