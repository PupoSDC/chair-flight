# Transfer of heat

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.01.02.03.01** -- Explain how local cooling or warming processes result
    in transfer of heat.
2.  **050.01.02.03.02** -- Describe radiation.
3.  **050.01.02.03.03** -- Describe solar radiation reaching the Earth.
4.  **050.01.02.03.04** -- Describe the filtering effect of the atmosphere on
    solar radiation.
5.  **050.01.02.03.05** -- Describe terrestrial radiation.
6.  **050.01.02.03.06** -- Explain how terrestrial radiation is absorbed by some
    components of the atmosphere.
7.  **050.01.02.03.07** -- Explain the effect of absorption and radiation in
    connection with clouds.
8.  **050.01.02.03.08** -- Explain the process of conduction.
9.  **050.01.02.03.09** -- Explain the role of conduction in the cooling and
    warming of the atmosphere.
10. **050.01.02.03.10** -- Explain the process of convection.
11. **050.01.02.03.11** -- Name the situations in which convection occurs.
12. **050.01.02.03.12** -- Explain the process of advection.
13. **050.01.02.03.13** -- Name the situations in which advection occurs.
14. **050.01.02.03.14** -- Describe the transfer of heat by turbulence.
15. **050.01.02.03.15** -- Describe the transfer of latent heat.
