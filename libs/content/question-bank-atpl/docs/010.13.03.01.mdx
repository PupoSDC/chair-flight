# Occurrences

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.13.03.01.01** -- Identify an occurrence as being either an accident,
    incident or serious incident in Regulation (EU) No 996/2010 of the European
    Parliament and of the Council of 20 October 2010 on the investigation and
    prevention of accidents and incidents in civil aviation.
2.  **010.13.03.01.02** -- Describe the relationship between Regulation (EU) No
    996/2010 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 20 October 2010 on
    the investigation and prevention of accidents and incidents in civil
    aviation and Regulation (EU) No 376/2014 of the European Parliament and of
    the Council of 3 April 2014 on the reporting, analysis and follow-up of
    occurrences in civil aviation.
3.  **010.13.03.01.03** -- State the subject matter and scope of Regulation (EU)
    No 376/2014.
4.  **010.13.03.01.04** -- Identify occurrences that must be reported.
5.  **010.13.03.01.05** -- Identify occurrences that should be voluntarily
    reported.
6.  **010.13.03.01.06** -- Describe how information from occurrences is
    collected, stored and analysed.
