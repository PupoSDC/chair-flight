# Landing procedures

## Learning Objectives

1.  **034.04.06.02.01** -- Explain the procedure for critical power-unit failure
    before and after the landing decision point.
2.  **034.04.06.02.02** -- Explain that the portion of flight after the landing
    decision point must be carried out visually.
3.  **034.04.06.02.03** -- Explain the procedures and required obstacle
    clearances for landings on different heliports/helidecks.
