# Ice and other contaminants

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.01.12.01.01** -- Describe the locations on an aeroplane where ice
    build-up will occur during flight.
2.  **081.01.12.01.02** -- Explain the aerodynamic effects of ice and other
    contaminants on: lift (maximum CL); drag; stall speed; alphaCRIT; stability
    and controllability.
3.  **081.01.12.01.03** -- Explain the aerodynamic effects of icing during
    take-off.
