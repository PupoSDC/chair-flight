# Electrical discharges

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.09.04.03.01** -- Describe the basic outline of the electric field in
    the atmosphere.
2.  **050.09.04.03.02** -- Describe types of lightning, i.e. ground stroke,
    intra-cloud lightning, cloud-to-cloud lightning, upward lightning.
3.  **050.09.04.03.03** -- Describe and assess the "St. Elmo"s fire" weather
    phenomenon.
4.  **050.09.04.03.04** -- Describe the development of lightning discharges.
5.  **050.09.04.03.05** -- Describe the effect of lightning strike on aircraft
    and flight execution.
