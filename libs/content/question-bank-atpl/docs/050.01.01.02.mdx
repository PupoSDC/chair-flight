# Troposphere

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.01.01.02.01** -- Describe the troposphere.
2.  **050.01.01.02.02** -- Describe the main characteristics of the tropopause.
3.  **050.01.01.02.03** -- Describe the proportions of the most important gases
    in the air in the troposphere.
4.  **050.01.01.02.04** -- Describe the variations of the FL and temperature of
    the tropopause from the poles to the equator.
5.  **050.01.01.02.05** -- Describe the breaks in the tropopause along the
    boundaries of the main air masses.
6.  **050.01.01.02.06** -- Indicate the variations of the FL of the tropopause
    with the seasons and the variations of atmospheric pressure.
