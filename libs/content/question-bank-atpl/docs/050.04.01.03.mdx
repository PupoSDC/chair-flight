# Influence of inversions on cloud development

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.04.01.03.01** -- Explain the influence of inversions on vertical
    movements in the atmosphere.
2.  **050.04.01.03.02** -- Explain the influence of an inversion on the
    formation of stratus clouds.
3.  **050.04.01.03.03** -- Explain the influence of ground inversion on the
    formation of fog.
4.  **050.04.01.03.04** -- Describe the role of the tropopause inversion with
    regard to the vertical development of clouds.
