# Speeds and Mach number

## Learning Objectives

1.  **082.02.01.01.01** -- Define the speed of sound in air.
2.  **082.02.01.01.02** -- State that the speed of sound is proportional to the
    square root of the absolute temperature (in Kelvins).
3.  **082.02.01.01.03** -- Explain the variation in the speed of sound with
    altitude.
4.  **082.02.01.01.04** -- Define Mach number.
5.  **082.02.01.01.05** -- Explain the meaning of incompressibility and
    compressibility of air; relate this to the value of the Mach number.
6.  **082.02.01.01.06** -- Define high subsonic, transonic and supersonic flows
    in relation to the value of the Mach number.
