  <Explanation>
    $$
    Convergency = dlong x sin mlat
    \= 18 x sin 60
    \= 18 x 0.866
    \= 15.6
    $$

    The track will change a total of 15.6º during the entire flight.

    $$
    Conversion angle = 1/2 convergency
    \= 1/2 x 15.6
    \= 7.8
    $$
