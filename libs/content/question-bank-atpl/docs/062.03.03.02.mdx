# Presentation and interpretation

## Learning Objectives

1.  **062.03.03.02.01** -- Explain the functions of the following different
    controls on the radar control panel:
    - off/on switch;
    - function switch with WX, WX plus T and MAP modes;
    - gain-control setting (auto/manual);
    - tilt/autotilt switch.
2.  **062.03.03.02.02** -- Name, for areas of differing reflection intensity,
    the colour gradations (green, yellow, red and magenta) indicating the
    increasing intensity of precipitation.
3.  **062.03.03.02.03** -- State the use of azimuth-marker lines and range lines
    in respect of the relative bearing and the distance to a thunderstorm on the
    screen.
