# Purpose, operation, components, location

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.16.07.01.01** -- Explain the purpose of a freewheel.
2.  **021.16.07.01.02** -- Describe and explain the operation of a: cam- and
    roller-type freewheel; sprag-clutch-type freewheel.
3.  **021.16.07.01.03** -- List the typical components of the various
    freewheels.
4.  **021.16.07.01.04** -- Identify the various locations of freewheels in power
    plant and transmission systems.
5.  **021.16.07.01.05** -- Explain the implications regarding the engagement and
    disengagement of the freewheel.
