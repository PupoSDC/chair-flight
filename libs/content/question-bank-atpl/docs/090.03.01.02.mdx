# Weather broadcast

## Learning Objectives

1.  **090.03.01.02.01** -- List the sources (VOLMET and ATIS units) of weather
    information available for aircraft in flight, and describe situation(s) in
    which a pilot would normally obtain each.
2.  **090.03.01.02.02** -- Explain the meaning of the acronyms "D-ATIS", "ATIS",
    and "VOLMET".
3.  **090.03.01.02.03** -- Explain and demonstrate how to decode ATIS messages.
4.  **090.03.01.02.04** -- Explain and demonstrate how to decode D-ATIS
    messages.
