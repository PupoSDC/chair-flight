# In-flight fuel management

## Learning Objectives

1.  **033.06.01.02.01** -- Explain why fuel checks must be carried out in flight
    at regular intervals and why relevant fuel data must be recorded.
2.  **033.06.01.02.02** -- Assess deviations of actual fuel consumption from
    planned consumption.
3.  **033.06.01.02.03** -- Calculate fuel quantity used, fuel consumption, and
    fuel remaining at navigation checkpoints/waypoints.
4.  **033.06.01.02.04** -- Compare the actual with the planned fuel consumption
    by means of calculation.
5.  **033.06.01.02.05** -- Determine the remaining range and endurance by means
    of calculation.
6.  **033.06.01.02.06** -- Calculate the revised fuel consumption based on
    changes to the pre-flight plan, including changes of W/V, cruise level, OAT,
    distances, Mach number and CAS.
