# Coning angle in the hover

## Learning Objectives

1.  **082.05.01.03.01** -- Define the tip path plane and the coning angle.
2.  **082.05.01.03.02** -- Show how the equilibrium of the moments about the
    flapping hinge of lift (thrust) and of the centrifugal force determine the
    coning angle of the blade (the blade mass being negligible).
3.  **082.05.01.03.03** -- Justify the lower limit of rotor rpm.
4.  **082.05.01.03.04** -- Explain the effect of the mass of a blade on the tip
    path and the tracking.
