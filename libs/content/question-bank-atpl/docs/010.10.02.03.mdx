# Entry and departure of passengers and baggage

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.10.02.03.01** -- Explain the entry requirements for passengers and
    their baggage.
2.  **010.10.02.03.02** -- Explain the requirements and documentation for
    unaccompanied baggage.
3.  **010.10.02.03.03** -- Identify the documentation required for the departure
    and entry of passengers and their baggage.
4.  **010.10.02.03.04** -- Explain the arrangements in the event of a passenger
    being declared an inadmissible person.
5.  **010.10.02.03.05** -- Describe the pilot"s authority towards unruly
    passengers.
