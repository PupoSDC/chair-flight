# ICAO Annex 18

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.02.12.01.01** -- Define the following terms: "dangerous goods",
    "dangerous goods accident", "dangerous goods incident", "exemption",
    "incompatible", "packaging", "UN number".
2.  **071.02.12.01.02** -- Explain that detailed provisions for the transport of
    dangerous goods by air are contained in the Technical Instructions for the
    Safe Transport of Dangerous Goods by Air.
3.  **071.02.12.01.03** -- State that in the event of an in-flight emergency,
    the pilot-in-command must inform the ATC of the transport of dangerous goods
    by air.
