# Characteristics of tropical revolving storms

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.07.04.01.01** -- State the conditions necessary for the formation of
    tropical revolving storms.
2.  **050.07.04.01.02** -- State how a tropical revolving storm generally moves
    in its area of occurrence.
3.  **050.07.04.01.03** -- Name the stages of the development of tropical
    revolving storms (tropical disturbance, tropical depression, tropical storm,
    severe tropical storm, tropical revolving storm).
4.  **050.07.04.01.04** -- Describe the meteorological conditions in and near a
    tropical revolving storm.
5.  **050.07.04.01.05** -- State the approximate dimensions of a tropical
    revolving storm.
6.  **050.07.04.01.06** -- State that the movement of a tropical revolving storm
    can only rarely be forecast exactly, and that utmost care is necessary near
    a tropical revolving storm.
