# Flight and duty time limitations and rest requirements

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.01.02.12.01** -- Explain the definitions used for the regulation of
    flight time limitations.
2.  **071.01.02.12.02** -- Explain the flight and duty time limitations.
3.  **071.01.02.12.03** -- Explain the requirements regarding the maximum daily
    flight duty period.
4.  **071.01.02.12.04** -- Explain the requirements regarding rest periods.
5.  **071.01.02.12.05** -- Explain the possible extension of flight duty period
    due to in-flight rest.
6.  **071.01.02.12.06** -- Explain that it is the captain"s discretion to extend
    flight duty in case of unforeseen circumstances in actual flight operations.
7.  **071.01.02.12.07** -- Explain the requirement regarding standby.
