# Attention and vigilance

## Learning Objectives

1.  **040.03.01.01.01** -- Differentiate between "attention" and "vigilance".
2.  **040.03.01.01.02** -- Differentiate between "selective" and "divided"
    attention.
3.  **040.03.01.01.03** -- Define "hypovigilance".
4.  **040.03.01.01.04** -- Identify the factors that may affect the state of
    vigilance.
5.  **040.03.01.01.05** -- List the factors that may forestall hypovigilance
    during flight.
6.  **040.03.01.01.06** -- Indicate the signs of reduced vigilance.
7.  **040.03.01.01.07** -- List the factors that affect a person"s level of
    attention.
