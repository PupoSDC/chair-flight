# Streamline pattern

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.01.04.02.01** -- Describe the general streamline pattern around the
    wing, tail section, and fuselage.
2.  **081.01.04.02.02** -- Explain and describe the causes of spanwise flow over
    top and bottom surfaces.
3.  **081.01.04.02.03** -- Describe wing tip vortices and their contribution to
    downwash behind the wing.
4.  **081.01.04.02.04** -- Explain why wing tip vortices vary with alpha.
5.  **081.01.04.02.05** -- Describe spanwise lift distribution including the
    effect of wing planform.
6.  **081.01.04.02.06** -- Describe the causes, distribution and duration of the
    wake turbulence behind an aeroplane.
7.  **081.01.04.02.07** -- Describe the influence of flap deflection on the wing
    tip vortex.
8.  **081.01.04.02.08** -- Describe the parameters that influence wake
    turbulence.
