# Fixed pitch and variable pitch/constant speed

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.07.01.04.01** -- List the different types of propellers: fixed pitch;
    adjustable pitch or variable pitch (non-governing); variable pitch
    (governing)/constant speed.
2.  **081.07.01.04.02** -- Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of
    fixed-pitch and constant-speed propellers.
3.  **081.07.01.04.03** -- Discuss climb and cruise propellers.
4.  **081.07.01.04.04** -- Explain the relationship between blade angle, blade
    angle of attack, and airspeed for fixed and variable pitch propellers.
5.  **081.07.01.04.05** -- Describe and explain the forces that act on a
    rotating blade element in normal, feathered, windmilling, and reverse
    operation.
6.  **081.07.01.04.06** -- Explain the effects of changing propeller pitch at
    constant IAS.
