# Cold front, associated clouds and weather

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.06.02.03.01** -- Define a "cold front".
2.  **050.06.02.03.02** -- Describe the cloud, weather, ground visibility and
    aviation hazards at a cold front depending on the stability of the warm air.
3.  **050.06.02.03.03** -- Explain the seasonal differences in the weather at
    cold fronts.
4.  **050.06.02.03.04** -- Describe the structure, slope and dimensions of a
    cold front.
5.  **050.06.02.03.05** -- Sketch a cross section of a cold front showing
    weather, cloud and aviation hazards.
