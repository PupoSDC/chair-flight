# Condition of the movement area and related facilities

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.09.02.04.01** -- State the purpose of informing AIS and ATS units
    about the condition of the movement area and related facilities.
2.  **010.09.02.04.02** -- List the matters of operational significance or
    affecting aircraft performance which should be reported to AIS and ATS units
    to be transmitted to aircraft involved.
3.  **010.09.02.04.03** -- Describe the different types of contaminant on RWYs.
4.  **010.09.02.04.04** -- Explain the different types of frozen water on the
    RWY and their impact on aircraft braking performance.
5.  **010.09.02.04.05** -- Describe the runway condition codes and the
    associated runway braking action.
