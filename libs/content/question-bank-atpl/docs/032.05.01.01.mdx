# Take-off (performance data)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.05.01.01.01** -- Determine from given graphs the field-lenght-limited
    take-off mass (FLLTOM) and describe situations in which this limitation
    could be most restrictive for take-off.
2.  **032.05.01.01.02** -- Determine from given graphs the climb-limited
    take-off mass and describe situations in which this limitation could be most
    restrictive for take-off.
3.  **032.05.01.01.03** -- Determine from given graphs the obstacle-limited mass
    and describe situations in which this limitation could be most restrictive
    for take-off.
4.  **032.05.01.01.04** -- Determine from given graphs the tyre-speed-limited
    take-off mass.
5.  **032.05.01.01.05** -- Determine from given graphs the maximum
    brake-energy-limited take-off mass.
6.  **032.05.01.01.06** -- Determine the take-off V speeds for the actual
    take-off mass.
7.  **032.05.01.01.07** -- Determine the maximum take-off mass using given RTOM
    tables.
8.  **032.05.01.01.08** -- Using RTOM tables, determine the take-off V speeds
    for the actual take-off weight using appropriate corrections.
9.  **032.05.01.01.09** -- Determine the assumed/flex temperature and take-off V
    speeds using the RTOM tables.
10. **032.05.01.01.10** -- Calculate the break cooling time following a rejected
    take-off given appropriate data.
