# Speed brakes and the reasons for their use in the different phases of flight

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.01.10.02.01** -- Describe speed brakes and the reasons for using them
    in the different phases of flight.
2.  **081.01.10.02.02** -- State their influence on the CL-CD graph and
    lift-drag ratio.
3.  **081.01.10.02.03** -- Explain how speed brakes increase parasite drag.
4.  **081.01.10.02.04** -- Describe how speed brakes affect the minimum drag
    speed.
5.  **081.01.10.02.05** -- Describe their effect on rate and angle of descent.
