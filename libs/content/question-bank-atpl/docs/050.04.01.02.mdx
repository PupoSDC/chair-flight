# Cloud types and cloud classification

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.04.01.02.01** -- Describe the different cloud types and their
    classification.
2.  **050.04.01.02.02** -- Identify by shape cirriform, cumuliform and
    stratiform clouds.
3.  **050.04.01.02.03** -- Identify by shape and typical level the 10 cloud
    types (general).
4.  **050.04.01.02.04** -- Describe and identify by shape the following species
    and supplementary features: castellanus, lenticularis, congestus, calvus,
    capillatus and virga.
5.  **050.04.01.02.05** -- Distinguish between low-, medium- and high-level
    clouds according to the World Meteorological Organization"s (WMO) "cloud
    etage".
6.  **050.04.01.02.06** -- Distinguish between ice clouds, mixed clouds and
    pure-water clouds.
