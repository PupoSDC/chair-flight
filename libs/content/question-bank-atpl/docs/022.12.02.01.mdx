# Annunciations, master warning, master caution, advisory

## Learning Objectives

1.  **022.12.02.01.01** -- State the annunciations given by the FWS and typical
    location for the annunciator(s): master warning; master caution; advisory.
2.  **022.12.02.01.02** -- Explain master warning: colour of annunciator: red;
    nature of aural alerts: continuous; typical failure scenarios triggering the
    alert.
3.  **022.12.02.01.03** -- Explain master caution: colour of the annunciator:
    amber or yellow; nature of aural alerts: attention-getter; typical failure
    scenarios triggering the alert.
4.  **022.12.02.01.04** -- Describe a typical procedure following a master
    warning or master caution alert: acknowledging the failure; silencing the
    aural warning; initiating the appropriate response/procedure.
5.  **022.12.02.01.05** -- Explain advisory: colour of the annunciator: any
    other than red, amber, yellow or green; absence of aural alert; typical
    scenarios triggering the advisory.
