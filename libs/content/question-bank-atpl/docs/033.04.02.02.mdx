# Update of navigation plan using the latest meteorological information

## Learning Objectives

1.  **033.04.02.02.01** -- Confirm the most fuel-efficient altitude from given
    wind, temperature and aircraft data.
2.  **033.04.02.02.02** -- Confirm true altitudes from given atmospheric data to
    ensure that statutory minimum clearance is attained.
3.  **033.04.02.02.03** -- Confirm magnetic headings and GSs.
4.  **033.04.02.02.04** -- Confirm the individual leg times and the total time
    en route.
5.  **033.04.02.02.05** -- Confirm the total time en route for the trip to the
    destination.
6.  **033.04.02.02.06** -- Confirm the total time from destination to the
    alternate aerodrome.
