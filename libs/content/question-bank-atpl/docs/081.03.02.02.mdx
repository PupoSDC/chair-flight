# Buffet onset

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.03.02.02.01** -- Explain the concept of buffet margin, and describe
    the influence of the following parameters on the concept of buffet margin:
    alpha; Mach number; pressure altitude; mass; load factor; angle of bank; CG
    location.
2.  **081.03.02.02.02** -- Explain how the buffet onset boundary chart can be
    used to determine: manoeuvrability; buffet margin.
3.  **081.03.02.02.03** -- Describe the consequences of exceeding MMO: light
    buffet, buffet onset.
4.  **081.03.02.02.04** -- Explain "aerodynamic ceiling" and "coffin corner".
5.  **081.03.02.02.05** -- Explain the concept of the "1.3g" buffet margin
    altitude.
6.  **081.03.02.02.06** -- Find (using an example graph): buffet free range;
    aerodynamic ceiling at a given mass; load factor and bank angle at which
    buffet occurs at a given mass, Mach number, and pressure altitude.
7.  **081.03.02.02.07** -- Explain why descent increases the buffet free range.
