# Properties and occurrence

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.09.05.01.01** -- Define "tornado".
2.  **050.09.05.01.02** -- Describe the formation of a tornado.
3.  **050.09.05.01.03** -- Describe the typical features of a tornado such as
    appearance, season, time of day, stage of development, speed of movement,
    and wind speed.
4.  **050.09.05.01.04** -- Compare the occurrence of tornadoes in Europe with
    the occurrence in other locations, especially in the United States of
    America.
5.  **050.09.05.01.05** -- Compare the dimensions and properties of tornadoes
    and dust devils.
