# Descending (descent performance)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.01.05.01.01** -- Resolve the forces during steady descent and in the
    glide.
2.  **032.01.05.01.02** -- Explain descent performance in relation to thrust
    available and thrust required (drag), and power available and power
    required.
3.  **032.01.05.01.03** -- Explain the meaning of "excess thrust required"
    (excess drag) and "excess power required" in a steady descent.
4.  **032.01.05.01.04** -- Interpret the "thrust/power required" and
    "thrust/power available" curves in a steady descent.
5.  **032.01.05.01.05** -- Explain the effect of mass, altitude, wind, speed and
    configuration on the glide descent.
6.  **032.01.05.01.06** -- Explain the effect of mass, altitude, wind, speed and
    configuration on the powered descent.
