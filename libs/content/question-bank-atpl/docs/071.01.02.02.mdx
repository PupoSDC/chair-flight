# General

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.01.02.02.01** -- Explain why CAT flights must meet the applicable
    operational requirements.
2.  **071.01.02.02.02** -- Define "flight manual limitations - flight through
    the height velocity (HV) envelope".
3.  **071.01.02.02.03** -- Define "helicopter emergency medical service (HEMS)".
4.  **071.01.02.02.04** -- Define "operations over a hostile environment -
    applicability". Explain that there are certain areas which should not be
    overflown and state possible sources of that information (e.g. governmental
    warnings, operator risk assessment).
5.  **071.01.02.02.05** -- Define "local area operations - approval".
6.  **071.01.02.02.06** -- Explain the requirements about language used for crew
    communication and in the operations manual.
7.  **071.01.02.02.07** -- Explain which are the operator requirements regarding
    the management system.
8.  **071.01.02.02.08** -- Explain which are the operator requirements regarding
    accident prevention and the flight safety programme.
9.  **071.01.02.02.09** -- Explain which are the regulations concerning the
    carriage of persons on an aircraft.
10. **071.01.02.02.10** -- Explain the operator"s and commander"s responsibility
    concerning portable electronic devices (PEDs).
11. **071.01.02.02.11** -- Explain the operator"s and commander"s responsibility
    regarding admission in an aircraft of a person under the influence of drug
    or alcohol.
12. **071.01.02.02.12** -- Explain the regulations concerning the endangerment
    of safety.
13. **071.01.02.02.13** -- List the documents to be carried on each flight.
14. **071.01.02.02.14** -- Explain the operator"s responsibility regarding
    manuals to be carried on board an aircraft.
15. **071.01.02.02.15** -- List the additional information and forms to be
    carried on board an aircraft.
16. **071.01.02.02.16** -- List the copies of items of information to be
    retained on the ground by the operator.
17. **071.01.02.02.17** -- Explain what responsibilities the operator and the
    commander have regarding the production of and access to records and
    documents.
