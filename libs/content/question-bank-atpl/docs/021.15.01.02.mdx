# Structural components and materials, stresses, structural limitations

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.15.01.02.01** -- Identify from a diagram the main structural
    components of the main types of rotor-head systems.
2.  **021.15.01.02.02** -- List and describe the methods used to detect damage
    and cracks.
3.  **021.15.01.02.03** -- Explain and describe the structural limitations to
    respective rotor systems, including the dangers of negative G inputs to
    certain rotor-head systems.
4.  **021.15.01.02.04** -- Describe the various rotor-head lubrication methods.
