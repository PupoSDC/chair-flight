# Surface charts

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.10.02.02.01** -- Recognise the following weather systems on a surface
    weather chart (analysed and forecast): ridges, cols and troughs; fronts;
    frontal side, warm sector and rear side of mid-latitude frontal lows; high-
    and low-pressure areas.
2.  **050.10.02.02.02** -- Determine from surface weather charts the wind
    direction and speed.
