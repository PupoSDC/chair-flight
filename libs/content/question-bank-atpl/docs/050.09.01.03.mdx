# Hazards of ice accretion, avoidance

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.09.01.03.01** -- State the ICAO qualifying terms for the intensity of
    icing.
2.  **050.09.01.03.02** -- Describe, in general, the hazards of icing.
3.  **050.09.01.03.03** -- Assess the dangers of the different types of ice
    accretion.
4.  **050.09.01.03.04** -- Describe the position of the dangerous zones of icing
    in fronts, in stratiform and cumuliform clouds, and in the different
    precipitation types.
5.  **050.09.01.03.05** -- Indicate the possibilities of avoiding dangerous
    zones of icing: in the flight planning: weather briefing, selection of track
    and altitude; during flight: recognition of the dangerous zones, selection
    of appropriate track and altitude.
