# Trim sheet for large aeroplanes

## Learning Objectives

1.  **031.05.02.04.01** -- Explain the purpose of the trim sheet and the methods
    to determine the CG position.
2.  **031.05.02.04.02** -- Check if the zero fuel mass CG or index is within the
    limits.
3.  **031.05.02.04.03** -- Determine the fuel index by using the "fuel index
    correction table" and determine the CG position as per cent MAC.
4.  **031.05.02.04.04** -- Check that the take-off mass CG or index are within
    the limits.
5.  **031.05.02.04.05** -- Determine "stabiliser trim units" for take-off.
6.  **031.05.02.04.06** -- Explain the difference between certified and
    operational CG limits.
7.  **031.05.02.04.07** -- Determine the zero fuel mass CG or index.
8.  **031.05.02.04.08** -- Explain the relationship between pitch control and CG
    position and the operational significance.
