# Descent techniques

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.04.05.01.01** -- Explain the effect of descending at constant Mach
    number.
2.  **032.04.05.01.02** -- Explain the effect of descending at constant IAS.
3.  **032.04.05.01.03** -- Explain the correct sequence of descent speeds for
    turbojet transport aeroplanes.
4.  **032.04.05.01.04** -- Determine the effect on TAS when descending in and
    above the troposphere at constant Mach number.
5.  **032.04.05.01.05** -- Describe the following limiting speeds for descent:
    maximum operating speed (VMO); maximum Mach number (MMO).
6.  **032.04.05.01.06** -- Explain the effect of a descent at constant Mach
    number on the margin to low- and high-speed buffet.
