# General rules - Collision avoidance - SERA

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.05.03.01.01** -- Describe the rules for the avoidance of collisions.
2.  **010.05.03.01.02** -- Describe the lights, including their angles, to be
    displayed by aircraft.
3.  **010.05.03.01.03** -- Interpret marshalling signals.
4.  **010.05.03.01.04** -- State the basic requirements for minimum height (HGT)
    for the flight over congested areas of cities, towns or settlements, or over
    an open-air assembly of persons.
5.  **010.05.03.01.05** -- Define when the cruising levels shall be expressed in
    terms of flight levels (FLs).
6.  **010.05.03.01.06** -- Define under what circumstances cruising levels shall
    be expressed in terms of altitude (ALT).
7.  **010.05.03.01.07** -- Explain the limitation for proximity to other
    aircraft and the right-of-way rules, including holding at runway (RWY)
    holding positions and lighted stop bars.
8.  **010.05.03.01.08** -- Describe the meaning of light signals displayed to
    aircraft and by aircraft.
9.  **010.05.03.01.09** -- Describe the requirements when carrying out simulated
    instrument flights.
10. **010.05.03.01.10** -- Explain the basic rules for an aircraft operating on
    and in the vicinity of an aerodrome (AD).
11. **010.05.03.01.11** -- Explain the requirements for the submission of an air
    traffic service (ATS) flight plan.
12. **010.05.03.01.12** -- Explain the actions to be taken in case of flight
    plan change or delay.
13. **010.05.03.01.13** -- State the actions to be taken in case of inadvertent
    changes to track, true airspeed (TAS) and time estimate affecting the
    current flight plan.
14. **010.05.03.01.14** -- Explain the procedures for closing a flight plan.
15. **010.05.03.01.15** -- State for which flights an air traffic control (ATC)
    clearance shall be obtained.
16. **010.05.03.01.16** -- State how a pilot may request ATC clearance.
17. **010.05.03.01.17** -- State the action to be taken if an ATC clearance is
    not satisfactory to a PIC.
18. **010.05.03.01.18** -- Describe the required actions to be carried out if
    the continuation of a controlled visual flight rule (VFR) flight in visual
    meteorological conditions (VMC) is not practicable any more.
19. **010.05.03.01.19** -- Describe the provisions for transmitting a position
    report to the appropriate ATS unit including time of transmission and normal
    content of the message.
20. **010.05.03.01.20** -- Describe the necessary action when an aircraft
    experiences a communication (COM) failure.
21. **010.05.03.01.21** -- State what information an aircraft being subjected to
    unlawful interference shall give to the appropriate ATS unit.
