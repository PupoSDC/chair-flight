# Drift-down

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.04.04.01.01** -- Describe the determination of en-route flight-path
    data with one-engine-inoperative in accordance with the CS-25 provision on
    en-route flight paths.
2.  **032.04.04.01.02** -- Describe the minimum obstacle-clearance height
    prescribed in the applicable operational requirements.
3.  **032.04.04.01.03** -- Describe the optimum speed that the pilot should
    select during drift-down.
4.  **032.04.04.01.04** -- Explain the influence of deceleration on the
    drift-down profiles.
