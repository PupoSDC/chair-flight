# Jet-engined and propeller-driven aeroplanes

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.08.02.01.01** -- Describe the effects on the aeroplane of asymmetric
    thrust during flight, for both jet-engined and propeller-driven aeroplanes.
2.  **081.08.02.01.02** -- Explain critical engine, and explain, for a
    propeller-driven aeroplane, the effect of the direction of propeller
    rotation.
3.  **081.08.02.01.03** -- Explain the effect of steady, asymmetric flight on a
    conventional (ball) slip indicator/turn indicator.
4.  **081.08.02.01.04** -- Explain the effect of a crosswind on asymmetric
    flight.
