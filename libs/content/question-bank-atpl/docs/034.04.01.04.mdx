# Take-off climb

## Learning Objectives

1.  **034.04.01.04.01** -- Define the segments of the take-off flight path.
2.  **034.04.01.04.02** -- Explain the effect of changes in the configuration on
    power and speed in the segments.
3.  **034.04.01.04.03** -- Explain the climb-gradient requirements for OEI.
4.  **034.04.01.04.04** -- State the minimum altitude over the take-off path
    when flying at the take-off safety speed in a Category A helicopter (VTOSS).
5.  **034.04.01.04.05** -- Describe the influence of airspeed selection,
    acceleration and turns on the climb gradient and best rate-of-climb speed.
