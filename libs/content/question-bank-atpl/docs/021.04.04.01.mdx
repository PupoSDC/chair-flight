# Types, structural components and materials, operational limitations, thermal plugs

## Learning Objectives

1.  **021.04.04.01.01** -- Describe the different types of tyres such as:
    tubeless; diagonal (cross ply); radial (circumferential bias).
2.  **021.04.04.01.02** -- Define the following terms:
    - ply rating;
    - tyre tread;
    - tyre creep;
    - retread (cover).
3.  **021.04.04.01.03** -- Explain the function of thermal/fusible plugs.
4.  **021.04.04.01.04** -- Explain the implications of and how to identify tread
    separation and wear or damage with associated increased risk of tyre burst.
5.  **021.04.04.01.05** -- Explain why the ground speed of tyres is limited.
6.  **021.04.04.01.06** -- Describe the following tyre checks a pilot will
    perform during the pre-flight inspection and identify probable causes: cuts
    and damages; flat spots.
