# Operational procedures (except preparation for long-range flight)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.01.02.04.01** -- Define the terms used for operational procedures.
2.  **071.01.02.04.02** -- State the operator"s responsibilities regarding the
    use of air traffic services (ATS).
3.  **071.01.02.04.03** -- State the operator"s responsibilities regarding
    authorisation of aerodromes/heliports by the operator.
4.  **071.01.02.04.04** -- Explain which elements must be considered by the
    operator when specifying aerodrome/heliport operating minima.
5.  **071.01.02.04.05** -- Explain what the operator"s responsibilities are
    regarding departure and approach procedures.
6.  **071.01.02.04.06** -- Explain which parameters should be considered in
    noise-abatement procedures.
7.  **071.01.02.04.07** -- Explain which elements should be considered regarding
    routes and areas of operation.
8.  **071.01.02.04.08** -- Explain the requirements for flights in reduced
    vertical separation minima (RVSM) airspace.
9.  **071.01.02.04.09** -- List the factors to be considered when establishing
    minimum flight altitude.
10. **071.01.02.04.10** -- Explain the requirements for carrying persons with
    reduced mobility.
11. **071.01.02.04.11** -- Explain the operator"s responsibilities for the
    carriage of inadmissible passengers, deportees or persons in custody.
12. **071.01.02.04.12** -- Explain the requirements regarding passenger seating
    and emergency evacuation.
13. **071.01.02.04.13** -- Detail the procedures for passenger briefing in
    respect of emergency equipment and exits.
14. **071.01.02.04.14** -- State the flight preparation forms to be completed
    before flight.
15. **071.01.02.04.15** -- State the commander"s responsibilities during flight
    preparation.
16. **071.01.02.04.16** -- State the rules for aerodrome/heliport selection.
17. **071.01.02.04.17** -- Explain the planning minima for instrument flight
    rule (IFR) flights.
18. **071.01.02.04.18** -- Explain the rules for refuelling/defueling with
    passengers on board.
19. **071.01.02.04.19** -- Explain the "crew members at station" policy.
20. **071.01.02.04.20** -- Explain the use of seats, safety belts and harnesses.
21. **071.01.02.04.21** -- Explain the requirements for securing passenger cabin
    and galley.
22. **071.01.02.04.22** -- Explain the commander"s responsibility regarding
    smoking on board.
23. **071.01.02.04.23** -- State under which conditions a commander can commence
    or continue a flight regarding meteorological conditions.
24. **071.01.02.04.24** -- Explain the commander"s responsibility regarding ice
    and other contaminants.
25. **071.01.02.04.25** -- Explain the commander"s responsibility regarding fuel
    to be carried and in-flight fuel management.
26. **071.01.02.04.26** -- Detail the rules regarding carriage and use of
    supplemental oxygen for passengers and aircrew.
27. **071.01.02.04.27** -- Explain the commander"s responsibility regarding
    approach and landing.
28. **071.01.02.04.28** -- Explain the circumstances under which a report shall
    be submitted.
