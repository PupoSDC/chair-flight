# Cause and development of tropical showers and thunderstorms: humidity, temperature, tropopause

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.08.02.01.01** -- State the conditions necessary for the formation of
    tropical showers and thunderstorms (mesoscale convective complex, cloud
    clusters).
2.  **050.08.02.01.02** -- Describe the characteristics of tropical squall
    lines.
3.  **050.08.02.01.03** -- Explain the formation of convective cloud structures
    caused by convergence at the boundary of the NE and SE trade winds
    (Intertropical Convergence Zone (ITCZ)).
4.  **050.08.02.01.04** -- State the typical figures for tropical surface air
    temperatures and humidities, and for heights of the zero-degree isotherm.
