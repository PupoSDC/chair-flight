# Reduction of visibility caused by precipitation and obscurations

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.09.09.01.01** -- Describe the reduction of visibility caused by
    precipitation: drizzle, rain, snow.
2.  **050.09.09.01.02** -- Describe the reduction of visibility caused by
    obscurations: fog, mist, haze, smoke, volcanic ash.
3.  **050.09.09.01.03** -- Describe the reduction of visibility caused by
    obscurations: sand (SA), dust (DU).
4.  **050.09.09.01.04** -- Describe the differences between ground and flight
    visibility, and slant and vertical visibility when an aircraft is above or
    within a layer of haze or fog.
