# Air traffic control (ATC) services

## Learning Objectives

1.  **010.07.01.04.01** -- Name all classes of airspace in which ATC services
    shall be provided.
2.  **010.07.01.04.02** -- Name the ATS units providing ATC services (area
    control service, approach control service, aerodrome control service).
3.  **010.07.01.04.03** -- Describe which unit(s) may be assigned with the task
    to provide specified services on the apron.
4.  **010.07.01.04.04** -- State the purpose of clearances issued by an ATC
    unit.
5.  **010.07.01.04.05** -- List the various (five possible) parts of an ATC
    clearance.
6.  **010.07.01.04.06** -- Explain why the movement of persons, vehicles and
    towed aircraft on the manoeuvring area of an AD shall be controlled by the
    aerodrome control tower (TWR) (as necessary).
