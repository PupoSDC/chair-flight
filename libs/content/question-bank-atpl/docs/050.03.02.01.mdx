# Condensation, evaporation, sublimation, freezing and melting, latent heat

## Learning Objectives

1.  **050.03.02.01.01** -- Define "condensation", "evaporation", "sublimation",
    "freezing and melting" and "latent heat".
2.  **050.03.02.01.02** -- List the conditions for condensation/evaporation.
3.  **050.03.02.01.03** -- Explain the condensation process.
4.  **050.03.02.01.04** -- Explain the nature of and the need for condensation
    nuclei.
5.  **050.03.02.01.05** -- Explain the effects of condensation on the weather.
6.  **050.03.02.01.06** -- List the conditions for freezing/melting.
7.  **050.03.02.01.07** -- Explain the process of freezing.
8.  **050.03.02.01.08** -- Explain the nature of and the need for freezing
    nuclei.
9.  **050.03.02.01.09** -- Define "supercooled water". (Refer to Subject 050 09
    01 01.
10. **050.03.02.01.10** -- List the conditions for sublimation.
11. **050.03.02.01.11** -- Explain the sublimation process.
12. **050.03.02.01.12** -- Explain the nature of and the need for sublimation
    nuclei.
13. **050.03.02.01.13** -- Describe the absorption or release of latent heat in
    each change of state of water.
14. **050.03.02.01.14** -- Illustrate all the changes of state of water with
    practical examples.
