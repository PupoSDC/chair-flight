# Minimum equipment list (MEL) and master minimum equipment list (MMEL)

## Learning Objectives

1.  **071.02.01.03.01** -- Describe the following terms: "commencement of
    flight", "inoperative", "MEL", "MMEL", "rectification interval".
2.  **071.02.01.03.02** -- Explain the relation between MMEL and MEL.
3.  **071.02.01.03.03** -- Define the "extent of the MEL".
4.  **071.02.01.03.04** -- Explain the responsibilities of the operator and the
    competent authority with regard to MEL and MMEL.
5.  **071.02.01.03.05** -- Explain the responsibilities of the flight crew
    members with regard to MEL.
6.  **071.02.01.03.06** -- Explain the responsibilities of the commander with
    regard to MEL.
