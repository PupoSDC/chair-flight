# The helicopter, characteristics and associated terminology

## Learning Objectives

1.  **082.03.02.02.01** -- Mention the tail rotor, the Fenestron, and the no
    tail rotor (NOTAR).
2.  **082.03.02.02.02** -- Define the rotor disc area and the blade area.
3.  **082.03.02.02.03** -- Describe the teetering rotor with its hinge axis on
    the shaft axis, and rotors with more than two blades with offset hinge axes.
4.  **082.03.02.02.04** -- Define the fuselage centre line and the three axes:
    roll, pitch, and normal (yaw).
5.  **082.03.02.02.05** -- Define gross weight and gross mass (and the units
    involved), disc and blade loading.
