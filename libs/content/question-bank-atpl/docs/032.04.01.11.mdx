# Take-off performance on wet and contaminated runways

## Learning Objectives

1.  **032.04.01.11.01** -- Explain the differences between the take-off
    performance determination on a wet or contaminated runway and on a dry
    runway.
2.  **032.04.01.11.02** -- Describe a wet V1 and explain the consequences of
    using a wet V1.
3.  **032.04.01.11.03** -- Describe the hazards, effects and management of
    operating from a contaminated runway.
4.  **032.04.01.11.04** -- Describe displacement drag, impingement drag, and the
    methods to monitor acceleration.
5.  **032.04.01.11.05** -- Explain the benefits and implications of using a
    derated take-off on a contaminated runway.
