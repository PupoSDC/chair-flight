# Spoilers and the reasons for their use in the different phases of flight

## Learning Objectives

1.  **081.01.10.01.01** -- Describe the aerodynamic functioning of spoilers:
    roll spoilers; flight spoilers (speed brakes); ground spoilers (lift
    dumpers).
2.  **081.01.10.01.02** -- Describe the effect of spoilers on the CL-alpha graph
    and stall speed.
3.  **081.01.10.01.03** -- Describe the influence of spoilers on the CL-CD graph
    and lift-drag ratio.
